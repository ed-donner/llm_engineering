# NaijaCode — Company Overview

## About
NaijaCode is a Nigerian technology company building developer tools for Africa. Founded in 2020 by Oluwaseun Adeyemi and Adaeze Okwu, the company's mission is to "empower every African developer to build world-class software."

## Headquarters
NaijaCode HQ is located in Yaba, Lagos, Nigeria — in the heart of what's known as "Yaba Left," Africa's most famous tech hub. The office is a converted warehouse on Herbert Macaulay Way.

## Offices
- **Lagos (HQ)**: 35 employees — Engineering, Product, Design, Operations
- **Abuja**: 5 employees — Sales and Partnerships
- **Nairobi (Kenya)**: 5 employees — East Africa expansion (opened 2024)

## Funding
- **Seed Round (2020)**: $500,000 from Microtraction and angel investors
- **Y Combinator (2021)**: $125,000 + network access
- **Series A (2023)**: $4.2 million led by Sequoia Africa, with participation from TLcom Capital and Future Africa

## Company Values
1. **Build for Africa First**: Every product decision starts with "Does this work for developers in Lagos, Nairobi, and Accra?"
2. **Ship Fast, Learn Faster**: We release early and iterate based on user feedback
3. **Community Over Competition**: We grow by growing the ecosystem
4. **Excellence is Non-Negotiable**: We hold ourselves to the highest standards

## Culture
- Remote-friendly with 2 days/week in-office for Lagos team
- Monthly "Demo Days" where any employee can showcase their projects
- Annual "NaijaHack" company-wide hackathon
- Free lunch on Mondays and Fridays (jollof rice is always on the menu)
- Learning budget of ₦200,000/year per employee

## Key Milestones
- **2020**: NaijaCode founded, first prototype of CodeNaija IDE
- **2021**: Y Combinator batch, launched CodeNaija IDE v1.0
- **2022**: Reached 50,000 users, hired employee #20
- **2023**: Series A funding, launched NaijaCode Marketplace, won TechCabal awards
- **2024**: Launched NaijaCode AI beta, opened Nairobi office, reached 120,000 users
