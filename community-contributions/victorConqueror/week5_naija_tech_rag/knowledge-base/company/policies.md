# NaijaCode — Policies and Benefits

## Working Hours
- Core hours: 9am - 4pm WAT (West Africa Time)
- Flexible hours outside of core time
- No meetings on Wednesdays ("Deep Work Wednesday")

## Leave Policy
- 25 days annual leave
- 10 days sick leave
- 3 months maternity leave (fully paid)
- 1 month paternity leave (fully paid)
- 5 days "Learning Leave" for conferences, courses, or certifications

## Benefits
- Health insurance (HMO) for employee + 2 dependents
- ₦200,000/year learning and development budget
- MacBook Pro or equivalent for all engineers
- Monthly internet stipend of ₦30,000
- Generator fuel allowance for Lagos-based employees (because NEPA no dey!)
- Free lunch on Mondays and Fridays
- Gym membership at any partner gym

## Remote Work
- Lagos team: Hybrid (3 days remote, 2 days in-office)
- Abuja and Nairobi teams: Fully remote with quarterly visits to Lagos
- Home office setup allowance of ₦150,000 (one-time)

## Performance Reviews
- Quarterly check-ins with manager
- Annual 360-degree review
- Promotion cycle: Every 6 months
- Salary review: Annual, benchmarked against Paystack, Andela, and Flutterwave

## Engineering Practices
- Code review required for all pull requests (minimum 1 reviewer)
- CI/CD pipeline must pass before merge
- On-call rotation: 1 week on, 3 weeks off
- Incident postmortems: Blameless, documented within 48 hours
- Tech stack decisions made by the engineering team, not management
