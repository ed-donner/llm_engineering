# Oluwaseun Adeyemi

## Role
CEO and Co-Founder

## Background
Oluwaseun "Seun" Adeyemi co-founded NaijaCode in 2020 alongside Adaeze Okwu. He studied Electrical Engineering at the University of Ibadan and later completed a program at Y Combinator in 2021. Before NaijaCode, he worked at Microsoft for 4 years in Redmond, Washington, on the VS Code team.

## Key Achievements
- Raised $4.2 million in Series A funding from Sequoia Africa and TLcom Capital in 2023
- Grew the company from 2 founders to 45 employees
- Named "Tech CEO of the Year" by TechCabal in 2023
- Built partnerships with MTN Nigeria and First Bank for developer programs
- Featured in Bloomberg Africa for his vision of "Africa's GitHub"

## Skills
Business Strategy, Fundraising, Public Speaking, Software Architecture, TypeScript

## Current Projects
Leading the company's expansion into Ghana and Kenya, and negotiating series B funding.

## Personal
Lives in Ikoyi, Lagos. Plays polo on weekends and is a huge Wizkid fan. Known for saying "If you can build for Nigeria, you can build for anywhere."
