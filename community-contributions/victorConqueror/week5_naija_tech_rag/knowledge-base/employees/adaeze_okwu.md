# Adaeze Okwu

## Role
Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

## Background
Adaeze Okwu is the co-founder and CTO of NaijaCode. She graduated from the University of Lagos with a First Class in Computer Science in 2012, then did her Masters at Carnegie Mellon. Before founding NaijaCode in 2020, she spent 6 years at Andela as a Senior Engineering Manager, where she built and led a team of 40+ engineers across Lagos, Nairobi, and Kigali.

## Key Achievements
- Led the development of NaijaCode's flagship product, CodeNaija IDE
- Architected the company's cloud infrastructure on AWS, reducing costs by 40%
- Won the "TechHer Nigeria" award in 2023 for outstanding women in tech leadership
- Published 3 papers on distributed systems at African tech conferences
- Speaks regularly at DevFest Lagos and PyCon Africa

## Skills
Python, Go, Kubernetes, AWS, System Design, Team Leadership

## Current Projects
Leading the development of NaijaCode AI — an AI-powered coding assistant trained on African developer patterns.

## Personal
Lives in Lekki, Lagos. Passionate about mentoring young women in STEM through the "SheCodesNaija" initiative she founded.
