# Fatima Bello

## Role
Head of Product

## Background
Fatima Bello joined NaijaCode in 2021 to lead the product team. She has an MBA from Lagos Business School and a BSc in Information Technology from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. Before NaijaCode, she was a Product Manager at Flutterwave where she helped launch Flutterwave Store.

## Key Achievements
- Grew NaijaCode's user base from 5,000 to 120,000 active developers in 2 years
- Launched the NaijaCode Community platform with 30,000+ members
- Introduced the freemium pricing model that increased revenue by 200%
- Named in "Forbes Africa 30 Under 30" in 2022
- Designed the NaijaCode onboarding flow that has a 78% completion rate

## Skills
Product Strategy, User Research, Data Analytics, Agile/Scrum, Figma

## Current Projects
Working on NaijaCode for Teams — an enterprise version of the platform targeting Nigerian banks and fintechs.
