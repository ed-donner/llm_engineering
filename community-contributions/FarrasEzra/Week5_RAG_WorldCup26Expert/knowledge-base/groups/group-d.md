## Outcome
The United States topped Group D on home soil. Australia claimed second
place after playing Paraguay to a scoreless draw on the final matchday.
Paraguay finished third but advanced anyway as one of the eight best
third-placed teams. Türkiye was eliminated.

The United States advanced to the Round of 32 to face Bosnia and
Herzegovina. Australia advanced to face Egypt. Paraguay advanced to face
Germany.
