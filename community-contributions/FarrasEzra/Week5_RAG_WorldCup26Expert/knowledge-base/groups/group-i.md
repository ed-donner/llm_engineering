## Outcome
France won Group I with a perfect record, the tournament's highest
group-stage points total alongside Argentina. Norway finished second,
its best World Cup group-stage showing in decades. Senegal finished
third but advanced as one of the eight best third-placed teams, edging
out Iran (Group G) on goal difference. Iraq finished bottom and was
eliminated.

France advanced to the Round of 32 to face Sweden. Norway advanced to
face Ivory Coast. Senegal advanced to face Belgium.
