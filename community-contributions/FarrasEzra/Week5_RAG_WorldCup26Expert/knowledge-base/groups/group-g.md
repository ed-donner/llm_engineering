## Outcome
Belgium claimed first place with a 5–1 win over New Zealand in the final
round. Egypt advanced as runner-up after a 1–1 draw with Iran. Iran drew
all three of its matches but finished third and did not qualify as one
of the eight best third-placed teams — pushed out on tiebreakers by
other third-place finishers — and was eliminated. New Zealand finished
bottom and was eliminated.

Belgium advanced to the Round of 32 to face Senegal. Egypt advanced to
face Australia.
