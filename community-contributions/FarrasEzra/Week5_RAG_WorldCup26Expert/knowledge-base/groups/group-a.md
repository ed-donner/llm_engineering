# Group A — 2026 FIFA World Cup

Teams: Mexico (host), South Africa, South Korea, Czechia

## Match results
- June 11: Mexico 2–0 South Africa (opening match of the tournament,
  Estadio Azteca, Mexico City)
- June 11: South Korea 2–1 Czechia
- June 18: Czechia 1–1 South Africa
- June 18: Mexico 1–0 South Korea
- June 24: Mexico 3–0 Czechia
- June 24: South Africa 1–0 South Korea

## Outcome
Mexico won the group with a perfect record. South Africa finished second.
South Korea finished third but did not qualify as one of the eight best
third-placed teams, and was eliminated. Czechia finished bottom and was
eliminated.

Mexico advanced to the Round of 32 to face Ecuador. South Africa advanced
to face Canada.
