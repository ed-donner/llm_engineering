## Outcome
Spain clinched first place with a 1–0 win over Uruguay in the final
round. Cape Verde locked up second place with a scoreless draw — its
third draw of the tournament, going unbeaten through the group stage
against Spain, Uruguay, and Saudi Arabia. Uruguay and Saudi Arabia were
both eliminated.

Spain advanced to the Round of 32 to face Austria. Cape Verde advanced
to face defending champion Argentina.
