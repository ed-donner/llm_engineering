## Outcome
Germany had already clinched first place ahead of the final round of
matches. Ivory Coast secured second place with a 2–0 win over Curaçao.
Ecuador upset Germany 2–1 in the final round but it wasn't enough to
finish in the top two — Ecuador still advanced as one of the eight best
third-placed teams. Curaçao, playing in its historic World Cup debut as
the smallest nation ever to qualify, finished bottom and was eliminated.

Germany advanced to the Round of 32 to face Paraguay. Ivory Coast
advanced to face Norway. Ecuador advanced to face Mexico.
