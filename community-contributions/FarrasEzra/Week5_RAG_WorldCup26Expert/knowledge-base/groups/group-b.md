## Outcome
Switzerland won the group. Canada finished second on home soil. Bosnia
and Herzegovina finished third but advanced anyway as one of the eight
best third-placed teams — its first successful qualification for a major
tournament since 2014, and its first trip beyond the group stage in this
tournament since 2014. Qatar finished bottom and was eliminated.

Switzerland advanced to the Round of 32 to face Algeria. Canada advanced
to face South Africa. Bosnia and Herzegovina advanced to face the United
States.
