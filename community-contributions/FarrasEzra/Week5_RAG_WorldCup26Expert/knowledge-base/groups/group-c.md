Teams: Brazil, Morocco, Scotland, Haiti

| Pos | Team | W | D | L | GF | GA | GD | Pts |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Brazil | 2 | 1 | 0 | 7 | 1 | +6 | 7 |
| 2 | Morocco | 2 | 1 | 0 | 6 | 3 | +3 | 7 |
| 3 | Scotland | 1 | 0 | 2 | 1 | 4 | -3 | 3 |
| 4 | Haiti | 0 | 0 | 3 | 2 | 8 | -6 | 0 |

## Outcome
Brazil finished first on tiebreakers, with Morocco level on points in
second. Scotland — playing at its first World Cup since 1998 — finished
third but did not qualify as one of the eight best third-placed teams
and was eliminated. Haiti went winless and was eliminated, its second
ever World Cup appearance (after 1974) ending at the group stage.

Brazil advanced to the Round of 32 to face Japan. Morocco advanced to
face the Netherlands.
