## Outcome
The Netherlands clinched first place with a 3–1 win over Tunisia in the
final round. Japan secured second place after a 1–1 draw with Sweden,
edging out Sweden for the automatic knockout berth. Sweden finished
third but advanced anyway as one of the eight best third-placed teams.
Tunisia went winless and was eliminated.

The Netherlands advanced to the Round of 32 to face Morocco. Japan
advanced to face Brazil. Sweden advanced to face France.
