## Outcome
England won Group L. Croatia finished second. Ghana finished third but
advanced anyway as one of the eight best third-placed teams. Panama
went scoreless across all three matches and was eliminated.

England advanced to the Round of 32 to face DR Congo. Croatia advanced
to face Portugal. Ghana advanced to face Colombia.
