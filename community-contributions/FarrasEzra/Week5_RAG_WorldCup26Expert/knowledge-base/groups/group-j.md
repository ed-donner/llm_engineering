## Outcome
Defending champion Argentina, led by Lionel Messi, won Group J with a
perfect nine points. Austria and Algeria finished level on points after
their final-round match ended in a 3–3 draw; Austria took second place
on the head-to-head/GD tiebreaker, while Algeria advanced anyway as one
of the eight best third-placed teams. Jordan, playing in its World Cup
debut, was eliminated after losing all three matches.

Argentina advanced to the Round of 32 to face Cape Verde. Austria
advanced to face Spain. Algeria advanced to face Switzerland.
