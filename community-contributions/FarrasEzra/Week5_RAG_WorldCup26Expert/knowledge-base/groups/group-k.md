## Outcome
Colombia finished first after playing Portugal (with Cristiano Ronaldo,
in what is expected to be his final World Cup) to a scoreless draw in
the final round. Portugal finished second. DR Congo finished third but
advanced anyway as one of the eight best third-placed teams — its first
appearance beyond the group stage since returning to the World Cup after
1974. Uzbekistan, in its World Cup debut, lost all three matches and was
eliminated.

Colombia advanced to the Round of 32 to face Ghana. Portugal advanced to
face Croatia. DR Congo advanced to face England.
