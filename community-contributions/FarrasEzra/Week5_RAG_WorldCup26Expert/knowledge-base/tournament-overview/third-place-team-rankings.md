# 2026 FIFA World Cup — Best Third-Placed Teams

After the group stage, the top two teams from each of the 12 groups
advance automatically (24 teams). The remaining 8 Round of 32 spots go to
the best-ranked third-placed teams across all 12 groups, ranked by:
1) points, 2) goal difference, 3) goals scored, 4) team conduct
(disciplinary) score, 5) FIFA world ranking.

## Summary
- Advanced as third-placed teams: Bosnia and Herzegovina, Paraguay,
  Ecuador, Sweden, Senegal, Algeria, DR Congo, Ghana.
- Eliminated as third-placed teams: South Korea, Scotland, IR Iran,
  Uruguay.
- All six teams with exactly 4 points as a third-place finisher advanced.
  Among 3-point third-place teams, only Senegal advanced (on goal
  difference), while South Korea, Scotland, and Iran were eliminated.
