# 2026 FIFA World Cup — Quarterfinals

The quarterfinals are played July 9–11, 2026. All four matches are
hosted in the United States.

## Thursday, July 9
**France 2–0 Morocco** — Gillette Stadium, Foxborough (Boston), MA.
Kylian Mbappé opened the scoring in the 60th minute (his 20th career
World Cup goal, equaling Messi's record) after missing a first-half
penalty; Ousmane Dembélé added a second in the 66th, his fifth goal of
the tournament. France advances to the semifinal against the winner of
Spain vs. Belgium. It is France's third straight World Cup semifinal
appearance, on track to become only the third nation (after Brazil in
1994/1998/2002) to reach three consecutive finals.

## Friday, July 10
**Spain 2–1 Belgium** — SoFi Stadium, Inglewood (Los Angeles), CA.
Spain won a close contest with a late goal from Mikel Merino, sending
Spain through to face France in the semifinal — a rematch of the 2024
UEFA European Championship semifinal, which Spain also won.

## Saturday, July 11

- **Argentina vs. Switzerland** — 9:00 p.m. ET, Arrowhead Stadium,

## Semifinal matchups (set)
