# 2026 FIFA World Cup — Round of 16 Results

The Round of 16 was played July 4–7, 2026.

## Saturday, July 4
- Morocco 3–0 Canada (Houston) → Morocco advances to the quarterfinals
  to face France
- France 1–0 Paraguay (Philadelphia) → France advances to face Morocco

## Sunday, July 5
- Norway 2–1 Brazil (East Rutherford, NJ) → Norway advances to face
  England
- England 3–2 Mexico (Mexico City) → England advances to face Norway

## Monday, July 6
- Spain 1–0 Portugal (Arlington, TX) → Spain advances to face Belgium
- Belgium 4–1 USA (Seattle) → Belgium advances to face Spain

## Tuesday, July 7
- Argentina 3–2 Egypt (Atlanta) → Argentina advances to face Switzerland
- Switzerland 0–0 Colombia, Switzerland won 4–3 on penalties
  (Vancouver) → Switzerland advances to face Argentina

## Notable results and context
- All three co-host nations were eliminated in the knockout rounds:
  Canada lost to Morocco in the Round of 16, Mexico lost to England in
  the Round of 16, and the USA lost to Belgium in the Round of 16.
- Morocco eliminated Canada 3–0 to reach a second consecutive World Cup
  quarterfinal.
- England came from behind to beat Mexico 3–2 in Mexico City.
- Switzerland eliminated Colombia on penalties after a scoreless draw.
- Argentina beat Egypt 3–2, with Lionel Messi involved in a dramatic
  Argentina comeback during the knockout rounds.

## Teams eliminated in the Round of 16
Canada, Paraguay, Brazil, Mexico, Portugal, USA, Egypt, Colombia

## The 8 teams that advanced to the quarterfinals
France, Morocco, Norway, England, Spain, Belgium, Argentina, Switzerland
