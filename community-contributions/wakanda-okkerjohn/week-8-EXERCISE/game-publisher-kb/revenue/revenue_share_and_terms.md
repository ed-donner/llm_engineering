# Revenue Share & Payment Terms

## Standard revenue share (Pixel Forge Games)

Unless otherwise agreed in the publishing agreement:

- **Steam:** After Valve's 30%, we split the remaining 70% as follows: **Developer 60%**, **Pixel Forge 40%** of the net (after platform cut).
- **Epic Games Store:** After Epic's 12%, we split the remaining 88%: **Developer 65%**, **Pixel Forge 35%** of the net.
- **Nintendo Switch / Xbox:** After platform share (typically ~30%), we split the remainder: **Developer 55%**, **Pixel Forge 45%** of the net.
- **GOG:** After GOG's share, we split net: **Developer 60%**, **Pixel Forge 40%**.

## Payment schedule

- **Frequency:** Royalties are calculated monthly and paid **60 days in arrears** (e.g. January sales paid by end of March).
- **Minimum payout:** No payment is sent until the developer's share for that period reaches **$500**. Amounts below that roll over to the next period.
- **Currency:** Payments are in **USD** unless otherwise agreed. We do not absorb conversion or transfer fees beyond our control.

## Advances

We may offer an advance against future royalties for selected titles. The advance is recouped from the developer's share of royalties before any further payout. Terms (amount, recoupment cap, milestones) are in the individual publishing agreement.

## What we cover vs what you cover

- **We typically cover:** Store page setup, standard QA (one round), basic marketing (store visibility, press list, key requests). For larger campaigns, we agree a budget and split in the contract.
- **You typically cover:** Development, original IP, music/asset rights unless we agree otherwise. Porting costs can be shared; see contract.

For exact numbers and exceptions, always refer to the signed publishing agreement for your title.
