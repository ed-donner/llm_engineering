# Team & Contacts

## Pixel Forge Games – Who to ask

- **Submissions (pitches, builds, first contact):** submissions@pixelforgegames.com  
  We respond within 6–8 weeks. Do not send multiple follow-ups in that window.

- **Contracts & revenue (signed deals, royalty statements, payouts):** contracts@pixelforgegames.com  
  For questions about revenue share, payment schedule, or your publishing agreement.

- **QA & release (build submission, lot check, store dates):** qa@pixelforgegames.com  
  For build handoff, platform submission status, and release scheduling.

- **Marketing (store assets, keys, press, trailers):** marketing@pixelforgegames.com  
  For capsule art, screenshots, copy, and visibility campaigns.

- **General / other:** hello@pixelforgegames.com  
  For anything that doesn’t clearly fit above; we’ll route internally.

We are a small team; please allow 3–5 business days for non-urgent replies. If you have a signed deal and an urgent blocker, say "Urgent" in the subject and we’ll prioritize.
