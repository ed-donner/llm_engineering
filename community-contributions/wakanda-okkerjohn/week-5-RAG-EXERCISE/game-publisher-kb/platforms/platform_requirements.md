# Platform Requirements Summary

Short reference for Steam, Epic, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox. Always confirm with the latest platform documentation.

## Steam (PC)

- **Store page:** Required. We create and manage; you provide assets (capsules, screenshots, description, trailer).
- **Build:** Windows (required); Mac/Linux optional. Steamworks SDK integration required.
- **Age ratings:** IARC or equivalent; we handle submission.
- **Revenue:** Valve takes 30%; remainder per our Revenue Share & Terms.
- **Key dates:** Build and store assets due at least 2 weeks before release. No strict first-come-first-served; we schedule with you.

## Epic Games Store

- **Store page:** We set up; you provide assets and metadata.
- **Build:** Windows. Epic’s distribution and SDK requirements apply.
- **Revenue:** Epic takes 12%; remainder per our Revenue Share & Terms.
- **Exclusivity:** We do not require Epic exclusivity unless separately negotiated and paid.

## Nintendo Switch

- **Developer / publisher:** We publish under our Nintendo publisher account. You need a development build that meets Nintendo’s technical requirements.
- **Lot check:** Nintendo performs lot check before release; we coordinate. Allow 2–4 weeks for first submission.
- **Revenue:** Nintendo’s share is typically around 30%; remainder per our Revenue Share & Terms. Payments from us on our standard 60-day arrears schedule.

## Xbox (Microsoft Store)

- **Certification:** Xbox requires certification (TRC compliance). We submit; you provide a compliant build and support during cert.
- **Revenue:** Microsoft’s share is typically around 30%; remainder per our Revenue Share & Terms.
- **Game Pass:** If we agree to a Game Pass deal, terms (lump sum vs usage-based) are in a separate addendum.

For submission checklists and TRC/requirements docs, ask the team (see Team & Contacts) or refer to the platform’s official developer portals.
