## Description
Fun and engaging dance sessions in Nairobi. Great for solo travelers looking to connect with local culture. The Stayez Dancing Classes experience connects guests with professional Kenyan dance instructors who teach a range of African dance styles — from traditional Kikuyu and Maasai dances to popular Afrobeats, Bongo Flava, and Amapiano moves.

## Dance Styles Available
- **Afrobeats:** High energy, modern West African style popular across Kenya
- **Amapiano:** South African house-influenced style, very popular in Nairobi
- **Traditional Maasai Dance:** Jumping and chanting rhythms — incredibly fun
- **Kikuyu Traditional Dance:** An authentic cultural experience
- **Bongo Flava:** Tanzanian R&B/hip hop fusion
