# Longonot Hike Experience

## Experience Details
- **Type:** Guided Outdoor Experience
- **Location:** Mount Longonot, Nakuru County
- **Price:** KSh 2,500 per person
- **Duration:** Full day (6-8 hours)
- **Best For:** Adventure seekers, nature lovers, hiking enthusiasts, team building

## Description
A thrilling guided hike up Mount Longonot volcano in the Great Rift Valley. Spectacular crater views await! Mount Longonot is a stratovolcano with a massive caldera at its summit. The hike climbs through savannah grassland, dense forest, and volcanic rock to the crater rim, which offers panoramic views of the entire Great Rift Valley and Lake Naivasha.

## What's Included
- Professional Kenyan Wildlife Service (KWS) park fees
- Experienced English and Swahili speaking guide
- Safety briefing and equipment check
- Park entry permits
- Certificate of completion
- Emergency first aid kit

## What to Bring
- Comfortable hiking boots or sturdy trainers
- Water (minimum 2 litres per person)
- Light snacks / lunch
- Sunscreen and sunhat
- Light rain jacket (weather can change quickly)
- Camera — the views are unforgettable!

## Getting There
Meet at the KWS park gate at Longonot town. Stayez can arrange private transport from Nairobi (2 hours journey). Pickup from Nairobi CBD available.
