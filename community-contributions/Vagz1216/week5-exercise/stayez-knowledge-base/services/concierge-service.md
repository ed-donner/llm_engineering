# Stayez Guest Concierge Services

## Service Details
- **Type:** Guest Support & Concierge Services
- **Location:** Nairobi, Kenya (available to all Stayez guests)
- **Price:** KSh 1,500 base (varies by service)
- **Availability:** 24/7 via WhatsApp

## Description
A range of personalized guest services available to enhance your stay — from airport pickups to in-stay concierge. Stayez's concierge team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp to help guests have the best experience possible in Kenya.

## Services Available

### Transport
- **Airport Pickup/Drop-off:** JKIA or Wilson Airport. Comfortable saloon car or SUV.
- **Nairobi City Tours:** Half-day or full-day guided tours with a local driver
- **Safari Day Trips:** Nairobi National Park, Lake Nakuru, Amboseli arrangements

### In-Property Services
- **Welcome Hamper:** Fresh tropical fruits, Kenyan coffee, snacks delivered to your property
- **Grocery Shopping:** Pre-arrival grocery stocking based on your dietary preferences
- **Chef Service:** Private in-unit chef for dinner (minimum 2 guests)
- **Laundry Service:** Same-day wash, dry, and fold

### Event Planning
- **Birthday Setup:** Balloons, cake, and decoration setup in your unit
- **Romantic Setup:** Rose petals, candles, champagne — perfect surprise for couples
- **Business Meeting Setup:** Projector, whiteboard, refreshments arranged

### Information & Recommendations
- Best local restaurants by cuisine type
- Nairobi nightlife recommendations
- Safari and nature experience bookings
- Cultural experiences and local guides
