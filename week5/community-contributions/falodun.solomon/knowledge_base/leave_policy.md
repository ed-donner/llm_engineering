Company Leave Policy

1. Purpose
This policy defines the types of leave available to employees and the procedures for requesting leave.

2. Scope
This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees.

3. Types of Leave

Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.

Sick Leave
Employees may take up to 10 days of sick leave per year.

Maternity Leave
Eligible employees receive 16 weeks of paid maternity leave.

Paternity Leave
Employees receive 10 working days of paid paternity leave.

Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all officially recognized public holidays.

4. Leave Request Procedure
Employees must submit leave requests through the HR system at least 3 days in advance.

5. Approval
Leave must be approved by the employee’s manager and HR department.

6. Leave Without Pay
If paid leave is exhausted, employees may request unpaid leave subject to approval.
