TRAVEL AND EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy outlines the procedures for reimbursing employees for business-related travel expenses.

2. Covered Expenses
Eligible expenses include:
- Airfare
- Hotel accommodation
- Ground transportation
- Business-related meals

3. Pre-Approval
All travel must be approved by the employee's department manager prior to booking.

4. Expense Submission
Employees must submit receipts and expense reports within seven days of completing travel.

5. Reimbursement Timeline
Approved expenses will be reimbursed within 14 business days.

6. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
Personal expenses, entertainment, and luxury services will not be reimbursed.
