EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING POLICY

1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to ensure a structured and welcoming onboarding process for new employees.

2. Orientation
New employees will participate in an orientation session during their first week.

3. Documentation
Employees must complete required documentation including tax forms, identification verification, and employment agreements.

4. Training
New employees will receive training on company systems, tools, and policies.

5. Probation Period
New employees are typically subject to a probation period of three months.
