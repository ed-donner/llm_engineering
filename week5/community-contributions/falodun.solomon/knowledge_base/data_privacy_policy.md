DATA PRIVACY POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy ensures the protection of personal and company data.

2. Data Handling
Employees must handle all personal and confidential data responsibly.

3. Access Control
Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel.

4. Data Storage
Sensitive data must be stored only in approved company systems.

5. Compliance
Employees must comply with applicable data protection laws and company privacy regulations.

6. Breach Reporting
Any suspected data breach must be reported immediately to the IT security team.
