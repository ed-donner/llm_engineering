PERFORMANCE REVIEW POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for evaluating employee performance.

2. Review Cycle
Performance reviews occur twice annually.

3. Evaluation Criteria
Performance evaluations consider:
- Job performance
- Achievement of goals
- Collaboration and teamwork
- Professional development

4. Feedback
Managers will provide constructive feedback and identify development opportunities.

5. Promotions
Promotion decisions may consider performance review results.
