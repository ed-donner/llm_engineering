REMOTE WORK POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy defines the conditions and expectations for employees working remotely.

2. Eligibility
Employees who have successfully completed their probation period may request remote work arrangements.

3. Remote Work Schedule
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week unless otherwise approved by management.

4. Availability
Employees must remain available during core working hours between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

5. Equipment
The company will provide necessary equipment such as laptops and secure access tools.

6. Data Security
Employees must follow all company IT security guidelines when working remotely and must ensure confidential information is protected.

7. Performance Expectations
Employees working remotely are expected to maintain productivity and communication standards equivalent to in-office work.
