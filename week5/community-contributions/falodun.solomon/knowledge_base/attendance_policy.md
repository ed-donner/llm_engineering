ATTENDANCE POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy establishes expectations regarding employee attendance and punctuality.

2. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

3. Absence Notification
Employees must notify their manager as soon as possible if they will be late or absent.

4. Excessive Absenteeism
Frequent or unexplained absences may result in disciplinary action.

5. Attendance Tracking
Attendance is tracked using the company's time management system.
