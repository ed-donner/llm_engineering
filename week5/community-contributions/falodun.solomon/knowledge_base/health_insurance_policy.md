HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy provides employees with access to company-sponsored healthcare coverage.

2. Eligibility
All full-time employees become eligible for health insurance after completing three months of employment.

3. Coverage
The health insurance plan covers:
- Medical consultations
- Hospitalization
- Prescription medication
- Preventive care services

4. Dependents
Employees may enroll spouses and children as dependents in the health insurance plan.

5. Claims Process
Employees must submit claims through the insurance portal within 30 days of treatment.

6. Coverage Changes
The company reserves the right to modify coverage terms as necessary.
