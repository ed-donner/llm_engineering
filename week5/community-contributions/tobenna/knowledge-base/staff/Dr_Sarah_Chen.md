## About Dr. Chen
Dr. Chen is passionate about helping patients manage chronic conditions through proper medication use and lifestyle changes. She leads CareFirst's Diabetes Wellness Program, which has helped over 200 patients improve their A1C levels since its launch. She also conducts monthly diabetes education workshops open to the community at no charge.

Outside of pharmacy, Dr. Chen enjoys trail running, cooking Taiwanese cuisine, and volunteering at the local free health clinic on weekends she's not working.
