## About Dr. Nguyen
Dr. Nguyen brings a clinical mindset to community pharmacy, treating every patient interaction as an opportunity to optimize medication outcomes. He conducts comprehensive medication reviews for patients on 5+ medications and has a particular focus on anticoagulation therapy, helping patients on warfarin maintain stable INR levels through regular monitoring and dietary counseling.

He also leads CareFirst's naloxone distribution program, providing free naloxone (Narcan) kits and education to patients, families, and community members at risk for opioid overdose.

David is a preceptor for pharmacy students from three local schools of pharmacy, hosting 4–6 students per year for community pharmacy rotations at CareFirst. He is passionate about training the next generation of clinical community pharmacists.

Outside of work, David enjoys photography, hiking the Appalachian Trail, and playing guitar in a local indie band.
