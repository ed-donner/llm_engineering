# Maria Lopez, CPhT

## Role
**Senior Pharmacy Technician & Bilingual Patient Coordinator**
CareFirst Pharmacy

## Background
Maria Lopez has been a cornerstone of the CareFirst Pharmacy team since 2016, making her the longest-serving team member. She is a Certified Pharmacy Technician (CPhT) who plays a vital role in prescription processing, inventory management, insurance billing, and serving as a bilingual resource for Spanish-speaking patients.

## Credentials
- Certified Pharmacy Technician (CPhT) — PTCB Certified, 2015
- Pharmacy Technician Diploma — Community College of Baltimore County, 2014
- Immunization-Trained Pharmacy Technician — 2022
- Advanced Insurance Billing Specialist (internal certification)
- CPR/First Aid Certified

## Responsibilities
- Prescription intake and processing
- Insurance claim submission and troubleshooting
- Prior authorization coordination with prescriber offices
- Inventory management and ordering
- Patient intake and profile management
- Spanish-language patient counseling support (alongside pharmacist)
- Training and mentoring new pharmacy technicians
- CareFirst Plus membership enrollment

## About Maria
Maria is often the first person customers interact with at CareFirst Pharmacy. She is known for her warm personality, patience with insurance issues, and dedication to making every patient feel welcome — especially those who are more comfortable communicating in Spanish. Maria has been instrumental in building CareFirst's reputation in the local Hispanic community.

She was recognized with CareFirst's Employee of the Year award in 2021 and 2023 for her outstanding patient service and contributions to the pharmacy's operations.

Maria enjoys spending time with her family, gardening, and volunteering at her church's community health fairs, where she helps with blood pressure screenings and health literacy education in Spanish.
