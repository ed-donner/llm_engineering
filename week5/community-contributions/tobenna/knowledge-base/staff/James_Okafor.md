## About James
James is known for his creativity in solving medication challenges. He has developed custom formulations that have made it possible for dozens of pediatric patients at CareFirst to take medications they previously refused due to taste. His compounding work also extends to veterinary patients — he prepares flavored medications for pets, making it easier for pet owners to administer necessary treatments.

James is an active member of the International Academy of Compounding Pharmacists (IACP) and regularly attends compounding conferences to stay current on techniques and regulatory changes.

In his free time, James coaches a youth soccer team, plays chess competitively, and is working on a blog about the art and science of compounding pharmacy.
