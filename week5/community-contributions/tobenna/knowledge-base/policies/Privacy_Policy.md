# Privacy Policy and HIPAA Compliance

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy
CareFirst Pharmacy is committed to protecting your personal health information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and all applicable state privacy laws.

## What Information We Collect
- Name, date of birth, address, phone number, email
- Insurance information and billing details
- Prescription history and medication records
- Allergy and health condition information (as reported by you or your prescriber)
- Payment information (credit/debit card numbers are encrypted and not stored on-site)

## How We Use Your Information
Your PHI is used only for:
- **Treatment:** Filling prescriptions, counseling on medications, coordinating care with your doctor
- **Payment:** Processing insurance claims, billing, co-pay collection
- **Healthcare Operations:** Quality improvement, staff training, regulatory compliance, fraud prevention

## When We May Share Your Information
We may share your PHI without your explicit authorization only in these limited circumstances:
- With your prescribing physician or healthcare provider for treatment coordination
- With your insurance company for claims processing
- As required by law (e.g., controlled substance reporting, public health reporting)
- For law enforcement requests with valid legal process
- To prevent a serious threat to health or safety

## Your Rights Under HIPAA
- **Right to access:** Request a copy of your pharmacy records at any time
- **Right to amend:** Request corrections to your records if you believe they are inaccurate
- **Right to restrict:** Request limitations on how your information is used or shared
- **Right to accounting:** Request a list of disclosures we've made of your PHI
- **Right to confidential communication:** Request that we contact you at a specific phone number or address
- **Right to a paper copy:** Receive a paper copy of this privacy notice at any time

## CareFirst Pharmacy Safeguards
- All electronic records are encrypted and stored on HIPAA-compliant systems
- Pharmacy consultations are conducted in a semi-private counseling area
- Staff undergo annual HIPAA training and sign confidentiality agreements
- Prescription labels are printed with minimal visible information on the outside of bags
- Fax transmissions to doctors include HIPAA-compliant cover sheets
- Disposed documents containing PHI are cross-cut shredded

## CareFirst Mobile App and Online Privacy
- App data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- Analytics data (non-identifiable) may be used to improve app functionality
- You can delete your app account at any time — associated data will be purged within 30 days
