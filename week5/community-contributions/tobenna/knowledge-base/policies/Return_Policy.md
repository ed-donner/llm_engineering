# CareFirst Pharmacy Return Policy

## General Return Policy
CareFirst Pharmacy accepts returns on most non-prescription products within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt. We strive to make the return process simple and hassle-free.

## Eligible for Return
- **Unopened OTC medications:** Full refund within 30 days with receipt
- **Unopened supplements and vitamins:** Full refund within 30 days with receipt
- **Health devices (blood pressure monitors, thermometers, etc.):** Unopened, within 30 days with receipt
- **Personal care products:** Unopened, within 30 days with receipt
- **Defective products:** Returned at any time with proof of purchase for exchange or refund

## NOT Eligible for Return
- **Prescription medications:** By law, once a prescription medication has left the pharmacy, it cannot be returned, resold, or redistributed — even if unopened and sealed. This is a federal regulation to ensure medication safety and integrity.
- **Opened OTC medications:** For safety reasons, opened medications cannot be returned unless defective.
- **Compounded medications:** Custom-prepared medications cannot be returned.
- **Refrigerated or temperature-sensitive products:** Cannot be returned once they leave the store (insulin, certain eye drops, probiotics requiring refrigeration).
- **Syringes, needles, and sharps:** Cannot be returned due to safety regulations.
- **Gift cards:** Non-refundable.

## Refund Methods
- **Original payment method:** Refund issued to the credit/debit card used or cash if paid in cash
- **No receipt:** Store credit issued at the current selling price, valid for 12 months
- **Insurance co-pay issues:** If you were overcharged on a co-pay, CareFirst will process a refund after verifying with your insurance — this may take 5–10 business days

## Defective or Recalled Products
- Products subject to manufacturer recall will be accepted at any time
- Defective products (broken seal, contamination, wrong contents) accepted at any time with or without receipt
- CareFirst will file a report and offer full refund or exchange

## Price Adjustments
- If an item goes on sale within 7 days of your purchase, bring your receipt for a price adjustment
- Limited to one price adjustment per item

## How to Return
1. Bring the product and receipt to the pharmacy counter or front register
2. A staff member will inspect the item and process the return
3. Refunds to credit/debit cards may take 3–5 business days to appear on your statement
