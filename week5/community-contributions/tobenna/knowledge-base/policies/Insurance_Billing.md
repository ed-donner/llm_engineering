## How Billing Works

### At the Counter
1. Present your insurance card at the pharmacy counter (new card or any updates)
2. Our system verifies your coverage in real time
3. You pay only your co-pay or coinsurance amount
4. CareFirst bills your insurance directly for the remainder

### Co-Pay Information
- **Preferred generics:** Typically $0–$15 co-pay
- **Non-preferred generics:** Typically $15–$30 co-pay
- **Preferred brand-name:** Typically $30–$60 co-pay
- **Non-preferred brand-name:** Typically $60–$100+ co-pay
- **Specialty medications:** Co-pay varies widely; ask our specialty team
- Actual co-pays depend on your specific plan — we verify at point of sale

### Prior Authorization
Some medications require prior authorization (PA) from your insurance:
- CareFirst pharmacy staff will notify you if a PA is needed
- We fax the PA request to your prescribing doctor on your behalf
- Typical PA turnaround: 24–72 hours
- We offer a limited emergency supply (up to 72 hours) for critical medications while PA is pending

## No Insurance?
CareFirst Pharmacy is committed to affordable medication access:
- **CareFirst Savings Card:** Free to join. Offers up to 80% off generic medications.
- **$4 Generic Program:** Over 100 common generic medications for $4 per 30-day supply, $10 per 90-day supply.
- **Manufacturer assistance programs:** Our pharmacists can help you apply for patient assistance programs (PAPs) offered by drug manufacturers.
- **We accept GoodRx, RxSaver, and SingleCare** discount cards for uninsured or underinsured patients.
- **Payment plans:** For expensive medications, ask about our interest-free payment plan option (for purchases over $100).

## Billing Disputes and Questions
- Review your receipt carefully — it shows the amount billed to insurance and your co-pay
- If you believe you were overcharged, bring your receipt and insurance card to the pharmacy counter
- Our billing specialists can re-process claims or contact your insurance on your behalf
- Refunds for billing errors are issued within 5–10 business days
