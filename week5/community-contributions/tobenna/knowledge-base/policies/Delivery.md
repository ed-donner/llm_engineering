# Delivery and Shipping Policy

## CareFirst Pharmacy Delivery Service
CareFirst Pharmacy offers convenient prescription and OTC product delivery for customers who cannot visit the store. Our delivery service covers a 15-mile radius from our location.

## Local Delivery

### Same-Day Delivery
- **Available:** Monday–Friday, for orders placed before 2:00 PM
- **Delivery window:** 3:00 PM – 7:00 PM same day
- **Cost:** $5.99 flat fee, FREE for orders over $25 or CareFirst Plus members
- **Coverage area:** Within 10 miles of the store

### Next-Day Delivery
- **Available:** Monday–Saturday
- **Cost:** $3.99 flat fee, FREE for orders over $15 or CareFirst Plus members
- **Coverage area:** Within 15 miles of the store

### How to Order Delivery
1. Call (555) 234-5678 and request delivery when placing or refilling your prescription
2. Use the CareFirst app — select "Deliver to Me" at checkout
3. Visit www.carefirstpharmacy.com/delivery
4. Ask at the pharmacy counter during your next visit to set up recurring delivery

### Delivery Details
- A CareFirst delivery team member will bring your order to your door
- **Signature required** for all prescription medications and controlled substances
- Someone 18+ must be present to accept prescription deliveries
- If no one is home, we will attempt redelivery the next business day or hold at the store
- Temperature-sensitive medications are transported in insulated bags with cold packs

## Mail-Order Service (Outside Local Area)
- **Available for:** Maintenance medications (90-day supplies)
- **Shipping:** USPS Priority Mail, typically arrives in 3–5 business days
- **Cost:** $7.99 flat rate, FREE for 90-day prescription orders
- **Controlled substances:** Cannot be shipped via mail per federal regulations — must be picked up in store or delivered locally
- To enroll: call (555) 234-5678 or visit the pharmacy counter

## CareFirst Plus Membership
- **$9.99/month** or **$99/year**
- Includes unlimited free local delivery (same-day and next-day)
- Free mail-order shipping on all orders
- Priority processing on all prescriptions
- 10% discount on OTC products
- Exclusive monthly wellness offers

## What Can Be Delivered?
- Prescription medications (new and refills)
- OTC medications and supplements
- Health devices (blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, etc.)
- Personal care products available at CareFirst

## What Cannot Be Delivered?
- Schedule II controlled substances via mail (local delivery OK with ID verification)
- Compounded medications requiring immediate refrigeration (available for local delivery only with cold chain packaging)
- Syringes and needles without an accompanying prescription delivery
