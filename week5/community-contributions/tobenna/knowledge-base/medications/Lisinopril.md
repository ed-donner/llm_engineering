# Lisinopril (Prinivil, Zestril)

## Overview
Lisinopril is an ACE inhibitor used to treat high blood pressure, heart failure, and to improve survival after heart attacks. It is among the top 5 most dispensed prescription medications at CareFirst Pharmacy.

## Classification
- **Type:** ACE Inhibitor (Angiotensin-Converting Enzyme Inhibitor)
- **Availability:** Prescription only
- **CareFirst Price:** $4.00 (30-count generic with CareFirst Savings Card)
- **Forms:** Tablets (2.5mg, 5mg, 10mg, 20mg, 40mg)

## Common Uses
- High blood pressure (hypertension)
- Congestive heart failure
- Post-heart attack therapy (improves survival)
- Diabetic kidney disease (nephropathy) protection

## Dosage Guidelines
- **Hypertension starting dose:** 10mg once daily
- **Heart failure starting dose:** 2.5–5mg once daily
- **Post-MI:** 5mg within 24 hours, then 5mg after 24 hours, then 10mg daily
- **Typical maintenance:** 10–40mg once daily
- **Maximum:** 80mg/day (hypertension), 40mg/day (heart failure)
- Can be taken with or without food; take at the same time each day

## Side Effects
### Common
- Dry, persistent cough (occurs in 5–20% of patients)
- Dizziness or lightheadedness (especially when starting)
- Headache
- Fatigue

### Serious (seek medical attention)
- Angioedema: swelling of face, lips, tongue, or throat — **call 911 immediately**
- Hyperkalemia (high potassium): muscle weakness, irregular heartbeat
- Severe hypotension: fainting, extreme dizziness
- Kidney function changes
- Liver problems (rare)

## Drug Interactions
- **Potassium supplements / potassium-sparing diuretics:** Risk of dangerously high potassium
- **NSAIDs (ibuprofen, naproxen):** May reduce blood pressure-lowering effect and increase kidney risk
- **Lithium:** Lisinopril can increase lithium levels
- **Aliskiren:** Contraindicated in diabetic patients
- **Sacubitril (Entresto):** Must not be taken within 36 hours of lisinopril

## Contraindications
- History of angioedema with any ACE inhibitor
- Pregnancy (can cause fetal harm — **Category D**)
- Bilateral renal artery stenosis
- Use with aliskiren in diabetic patients
- Breastfeeding (use with caution)

## Storage
- Store at room temperature (68–77°F / 20–25°C)
- Protect from moisture and light
- Keep out of reach of children
