# Health Screening Services

## Overview
CareFirst Pharmacy offers convenient, affordable health screenings to help you monitor your health between doctor visits. No appointment is needed for most screenings — walk in during screening hours.

## Available Screenings

### Blood Pressure Check
- **Cost:** FREE (always, for everyone)
- **How:** Sit at our in-store wellness station or ask any pharmacist
- **Results:** Immediate. We provide a printed result and can log it in your CareFirst health profile.
- **Availability:** Any time during pharmacy hours

### Blood Glucose Screening
- **Cost:** $10 (free for CareFirst Plus members and Diabetes Wellness Program enrollees)
- **How:** Simple finger-prick test at the pharmacy counter
- **Results:** Immediate. Fasting glucose and random glucose both available.
- **Recommended for:** Adults over 45, overweight individuals, those with family history of diabetes, women with history of gestational diabetes
- **Availability:** Walk-in during pharmacy hours

### Cholesterol Screening (Lipid Panel)
- **Cost:** $25 (includes total cholesterol, HDL, LDL, triglycerides)
- **How:** Finger-prick point-of-care test
- **Preparation:** 9–12 hour fast recommended for most accurate results (water and medications are OK)
- **Results:** Available within 5 minutes
- **Availability:** Walk-in screening days: first Saturday of each month, 9am–1pm. By appointment other days.

### A1C Testing (Hemoglobin A1C)
- **Cost:** $20 (free for Diabetes Wellness Program enrollees)
- **How:** Finger-prick test, no fasting required
- **Results:** Available within 5 minutes
- **What it measures:** Average blood sugar control over the past 2–3 months
- **Recommended for:** Diabetic and prediabetic patients (recommended every 3–6 months)
- **Availability:** Walk-in during pharmacy hours

### BMI and Body Composition
- **Cost:** FREE
- **How:** Height, weight, and waist circumference measured at our wellness station
- **Results:** Immediate BMI calculation with interpretation
- **Availability:** Any time during pharmacy hours

### Bone Density Screening
- **Cost:** $35
- **How:** Ultrasound-based heel scan (non-invasive, no radiation)
- **Results:** Available within minutes; referral for full DEXA scan if indicated
- **Recommended for:** Women 65+, men 70+, postmenopausal women with risk factors
- **Availability:** By appointment or on screening event days (quarterly)

## CareFirst Wellness Events
CareFirst Pharmacy hosts community wellness events throughout the year:
- **Monthly:** Free blood pressure and BMI checks (first Saturday)
- **Quarterly:** Comprehensive screening day (blood pressure + glucose + cholesterol + A1C at a bundled discount of $40)
- **Annually:** Heart Health Month (February), Diabetes Awareness Month (November) — special pricing and educational events
- **Flu Season (Sept–Mar):** Walk-in flu shot clinics with free blood pressure checks

## What Happens with Your Results?
- Results are explained by a CareFirst pharmacist on the spot
- If results are abnormal, we recommend follow-up with your primary care physician
- With your permission, we can fax results directly to your doctor
- Results are stored in your CareFirst health profile (if you opt in) for trend tracking
- **Important:** Pharmacy screenings are for monitoring purposes and do not replace diagnosis from a physician
