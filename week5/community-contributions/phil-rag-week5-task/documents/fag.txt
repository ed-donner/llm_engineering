Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What are the working hours for employees?
The standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are expected to log into the company system at the beginning of their shift.

2. How many leave days are employees entitled to each year?
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave requests must be submitted in advance through the HR management system.

3. What should I do if I forget my system password?
If you forget your password, use the password reset option on the company login page. If the issue persists, contact the IT support team for assistance.

4. Can employees work remotely?
Yes, employees may work remotely up to two days per week with the approval of their direct manager.

5. How do I request technical support?
Technical support requests can be submitted through the internal IT helpdesk portal. You may also contact the IT department via email for urgent issues.

6. What is the company policy on data security?
Employees must follow all cybersecurity guidelines, including using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and ensuring company data is not shared with unauthorized individuals.

7. Where can I find company policies and guidelines?
All official policies, manuals, and guidelines are available on the company intranet under the Human Resources section.

8. Who should I contact for HR-related issues?
For HR-related inquiries such as leave requests, payroll, or employee benefits, contact the Human Resources department through the HR portal.

9. Are training programs available for employees?
Yes, the company provides regular training programs to help employees improve their technical and professional skills.

10. What should I do if I experience technical issues with company software?
If you encounter any technical problems, report them through the IT helpdesk system or contact the IT support team immediately.
