# Samsung Galaxy Z Fold and Z Flip

**Galaxy Z Fold 6**: Foldable flagship. 6.3" cover display, 7.6" inner foldable Dynamic AMOLED 2X, 120Hz. Snapdragon 8 Gen 3. 12GB RAM, 256/512GB/1TB. Triple cameras: 50MP main, 12MP ultrawide, 10MP telephoto. 4400mAh battery, 25W wired, 15W wireless. S Pen Fold Edition support. Flex Mode for hands-free video and apps. Best for multitasking and productivity.

**Galaxy Z Flip 6**: Clamshell foldable. 3.4" cover display, 6.7" inner FHD+ 120Hz. Snapdragon 8 Gen 3. 12GB RAM, 256/512GB. Dual cameras: 50MP main, 12MP ultrawide. 4000mAh battery, 25W wired, 15W wireless. FlexCam for selfies and video. Compact when folded, fits in small pockets. Popular for style and portability.

Both Z series phones have IP48 water resistance. One UI on foldables includes split-screen, App Pair, and taskbar for quick app switching.
