# Samsung Galaxy A Series

The Galaxy A series offers mid-range and budget Galaxy phones with good value.

**Galaxy A55**: 6.6" Super AMOLED 120Hz. Exynos 1480. 8GB RAM, 128/256GB, microSD. Triple camera: 50MP main, 12MP ultrawide, 5MP macro. 5000mAh, 25W charging. IP67. One UI 6.1, 4 OS updates. Metal frame, glass back. Strong mid-range option.

**Galaxy A35**: 6.6" Super AMOLED 120Hz. Exynos 1380. 6/8GB RAM, 128/256GB. 50MP main, 8MP ultrawide, 5MP macro. 5000mAh, 25W. IP67. Good for everyday use and battery life.

**Galaxy A15 / A25**: Entry-level. 6.5" display, MediaTek or Exynos, 4–8GB RAM. 50MP main camera. 5000mAh battery. One UI Core. Affordable way to get Samsung experience and long battery.

A series shares One UI features like Knox security, Samsung Wallet, and link to Windows. No wireless charging or S Pen on A models.
