# Samsung Galaxy Features and Software

**One UI**: Samsung's Android skin. One UI 6 and 6.1 offer Quick Panel, lock screen customization, Good Lock modules, and consistent design across Galaxy phones and tablets. Multi-window, pop-up view, and edge panels for productivity.

**Galaxy AI** (S24 and newer): Circle to Search (search from any screen with a gesture), Live Translate (real-time call and in-app translation), Photo Assist (edit and generative fill), Note Assist (summarize and format notes), Transcript Assist. Some features require login and data processing.

**Samsung Knox**: Hardware-backed security. Secure Folder, Samsung Pass, Knox Vault for sensitive data. Used by many enterprises for work devices.

**DeX**: Turn your Galaxy into a desktop-like experience on a monitor or PC. Wireless or wired. Good for productivity and presentations.

**Battery and charging**: Most Galaxies have 25W or 45W wired (USB-C), 15W wireless, and Wireless PowerShare to charge other devices. Adaptive battery and power-saving modes extend usage.

**Updates**: Flagship S and Z series get 7 years of OS and security updates. A series typically 4–5 years. Security patches monthly or quarterly depending on model.
