# Samsung Galaxy S24 Series

The Galaxy S24, S24+, and S24 Ultra are Samsung's flagship smartphones (2024).

**Galaxy S24**: 6.2" Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, 120Hz. Chipset: Exynos 2400 (international) or Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (US). 8GB RAM, 128/256GB storage. Cameras: 50MP main, 12MP ultrawide, 10MP telephoto 3x. 4000mAh battery, 25W wired, 15W wireless charging. IP68, One UI 6.1, 7 years of updates.

**Galaxy S24+**: 6.7" display, same chipset options. 12GB RAM, 256/512GB. Same camera setup. 4900mAh battery, 45W wired charging. Titanium frame on select colors.

**Galaxy S24 Ultra**: 6.8" flat Dynamic AMOLED 2X, 120Hz. Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 only. 12GB RAM, 256/512GB/1TB. Cameras: 200MP main, 12MP ultrawide, 50MP 5x telephoto, 10MP 3x telephoto. 5000mAh, 45W wired, 15W wireless. S Pen included. Titanium build. Best for power users and stylus use.

All S24 models support Galaxy AI features: Circle to Search, Live Translate, Photo Assist, Note Assist.
