# Mango

## Overview
Mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical stone fruit native to South Asia but now cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions around the world. Known for its sweet and juicy flesh, this vibrant fruit varies in size, color, and flavor depending on the variety. Mangoes are not only prized for their delicious taste but also for their nutritional benefits and versatility in culinary applications.

## Health Benefits
- Rich in antioxidants, which may help in protecting against oxidative stress.
- Supports digestive health due to its fiber content.
- May enhance skin health and promote a youthful appearance thanks to high vitamin A and C levels.
- May boost immunity owing to its high vitamin C content.
- Contains compounds that may aid in heart health by improving cholesterol levels.

## Common Uses
- Eaten raw as a snack or dessert.
- Used in smoothies, juices, and fruit salads.
- Incorporated into savory dishes, curries, and salsas.
- Utilized for flavoring desserts such as ice creams and puddings.
- Employed in skincare products for its hydrating and rejuvenating properties.
