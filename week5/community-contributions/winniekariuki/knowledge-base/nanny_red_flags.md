# Red Flags When Hiring a Nanny or Househelp

Certain behaviors during the hiring process may indicate potential risk.

## Identity Verification Red Flags

- Refusal to provide a national ID
- Providing inconsistent identity details
- Providing someone else's identification

## Reference Red Flags

- Refusal to provide references
- Only providing relatives as references
- Previous employers unwilling to discuss employment

## Employment History Red Flags

- Frequent job changes in short periods
- Leaving jobs without explanation
- Inconsistent work history

## Behavioral Warning Signs

- Becoming defensive when asked verification questions
- Avoiding discussion about previous employment
- Changing phone numbers frequently

Employers should investigate these issues before hiring.
