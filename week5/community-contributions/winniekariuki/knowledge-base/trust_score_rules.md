# SafeHire Trust Score Guidelines

SafeHire evaluates domestic workers using verification indicators.

## High Trust Indicators

A worker may be considered high trust if they have:

- Verified national ID
- Two or more references from previous employers
- Stable employment history (1 year or more per job)
- Positive feedback from references

## Medium Trust Indicators

Moderate trust situations include:

- Only one reference
- Partial employment verification
- Limited employment history

## High Risk Indicators

High risk indicators include:

- Refusal to provide ID
- No references
- Frequent job changes
- Negative employer feedback

Employers should carefully review workers who meet high risk indicators.
