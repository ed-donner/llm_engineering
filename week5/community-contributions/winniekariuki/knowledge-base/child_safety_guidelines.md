# Child Safety Guidelines for Domestic Workers

Domestic workers caring for children must follow basic safety standards.

## Essential Safety Practices

A nanny should:

- Never leave a baby unattended
- Follow feeding instructions provided by parents
- Maintain hygiene when preparing food
- Immediately report injuries or illness

## Supervision Expectations

Employers should:

- Observe the nanny during the first weeks of employment
- Install basic monitoring systems if necessary
- Maintain regular communication

## Warning Signs of Unsafe Care

Parents should watch for:

- Children appearing fearful around the nanny
- Frequent unexplained injuries
- Changes in child behavior

These signs may require immediate investigation.
