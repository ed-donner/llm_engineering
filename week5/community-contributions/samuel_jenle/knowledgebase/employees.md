# NexaCore Employees

NexaCore employs 210 people globally as of 2025. The company operates a hybrid-remote model, with employees distributed across five office locations and a significant remote workforce.

---

## Leadership Team

### Dr. Priya Nair — Co-Founder & CEO
Priya holds a PhD in Computer Science from MIT and previously led the automation division at Palantir. She drives NexaCore's overall vision and go-to-market strategy. Based in Austin, TX.

### Marcus Webb — Co-Founder & CTO
Marcus is a serial entrepreneur who previously co-founded and sold DevGrid (acquired by Atlassian in 2015). He oversees all engineering and product development. Based in Austin, TX.

### Sofia Reyes — Chief Revenue Officer
Sofia joined NexaCore in 2021 from Salesforce, where she spent eight years in enterprise sales leadership. She manages global sales, partnerships, and customer success. Based in Chicago, IL.

### James Okoro — Chief Financial Officer
James was previously VP of Finance at Zendesk. He joined NexaCore ahead of the Series C in 2023 to lead financial planning and investor relations. Based in Austin, TX.

### Lena Hoffmann — Chief Product Officer
Lena joined in 2022 from SAP. She oversees the product roadmap across all six platforms and manages the product and design teams. Based in Lisbon, Portugal.

### Raj Patel — VP of Engineering
Raj leads the core engineering org of 85 engineers across Austin and Lisbon. He previously worked at AWS as a Principal Engineer. Based in Lisbon, Portugal.

### Amara Diallo — VP of Customer Success
Amara manages a team of 18 customer success managers globally, ensuring client retention and expansion. Previously at HubSpot. Based in Toronto, Canada.

### Tom Hargreaves — VP of Marketing
Tom leads brand, demand generation, and content marketing. Former Head of Marketing at Intercom. Based in London, UK.

## Hiring & Culture

NexaCore has been recognized on the Austin Business Journal's "Best Places to Work" list in 2023 and 2024. The company offers:
- Fully remote-eligible roles for engineering and product
- Equity participation for all full-time employees
- $2,000 annual learning & development stipend
- Unlimited PTO with a mandatory minimum of 15 days
- Quarterly all-hands retreats rotating between office locations
