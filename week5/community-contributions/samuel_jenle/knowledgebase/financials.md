## Financial Outlook (FY2025 Projections)

NexaCore is targeting $115M ARR by end of FY2025, driven by:
- Expansion of the NexaAssist product into 15+ new enterprise accounts
- Upsell motion on existing FlowCore clients into DataBridge and InsightIQ
- Entry into the Australian and DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland) markets
- Strategic partnership with Deloitte as a certified implementation partner

The company is targeting cash-flow breakeven by Q4 2025 and is exploring a potential IPO or strategic acquisition in 2026–2027.
