## Notable Contracts

### Meridian Logistics Group — FlowCore + DataBridge
**Sector:** Logistics  
**Contract Value:** $18,000/month  
**Since:** 2019  
Meridian was one of NexaCore's earliest enterprise clients. They use FlowCore to automate freight dispatching workflows and DataBridge to sync data between their warehouse management system and ERP. This is NexaCore's longest-standing client relationship.

### BlueCross Midwest Health — FlowCore + ComplianceGuard
**Sector:** Healthcare Administration  
**Contract Value:** $22,000/month  
**Since:** 2022  
BlueCross Midwest adopted FlowCore for prior authorization workflows and ComplianceGuard for HIPAA compliance monitoring. The contract expanded in 2024 to include a ComplianceGuard GDPR module for their international operations.

### Pinnacle Financial Partners — DataBridge + InsightIQ + ComplianceGuard
**Sector:** Financial Services  
**Contract Value:** $31,000/month  
**Since:** 2023  
Pinnacle is NexaCore's highest-value contract. They use all three products for real-time trading data pipelines, executive dashboards, and SOX compliance automation. A renewal and expansion negotiation is scheduled for Q3 2025.

### RetailMax Corp — FlowCore + TalentFlow + InsightIQ
**Sector:** Retail  
**Contract Value:** $14,500/month  
**Since:** 2023  
RetailMax uses FlowCore for supply chain approval workflows, TalentFlow for seasonal workforce onboarding, and InsightIQ for store performance dashboards across 400+ locations.

### GreenPath Energy — NexaAssist + FlowCore
**Sector:** Energy  
**Contract Value:** $8,200/month  
**Since:** 2024  
GreenPath is one of NexaAssist's first enterprise clients, using the AI helpdesk to surface internal maintenance SOPs and safety procedures for field technicians. FlowCore handles maintenance ticket routing.

## Contract Terms & Policies

- **Standard Term:** 12 months, auto-renewing
- **Multi-year Discount:** 10% for 2-year, 18% for 3-year commitments
- **Payment Terms:** Net-30, billed annually in advance
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime guarantee with financial credits for breaches
- **Data Residency:** US, EU, and Canada regions available; GDPR-compliant EU hosting included for European clients
- **Termination Clause:** 90-day written notice required for non-renewal
- **Professional Services:** Available at $225/hour or bundled implementation packages from $8,000
