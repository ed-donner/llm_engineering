# About NexaCore

NexaCore was founded in 2017 by Dr. Priya Nair and Marcus Webb in Austin, Texas, as a B2B SaaS company focused on AI-powered workflow automation for mid-market enterprises. The company's founding vision was simple: eliminate the manual, repetitive operations that drain productivity from growing businesses.

NexaCore's first product, **FlowCore**, launched in 2018 as a drag-and-drop workflow builder that integrated with existing enterprise tools like Salesforce, SAP, and Microsoft 365. It gained rapid traction in the logistics and healthcare administration sectors, winning NexaCore its first 15 enterprise clients within 18 months.

By 2021, NexaCore had grown to 180 employees across offices in Austin (HQ), Chicago, Toronto, and London. Revenue surpassed $42M ARR as the company expanded its platform into a full suite of automation and analytics products.

In 2023, NexaCore underwent a focused growth strategy, doubling down on AI integration across all products. The company raised a $75M Series C led by Horizon Ventures and used the capital to accelerate R&D, expand its sales team, and open a new engineering hub in Lisbon, Portugal.

As of 2025, NexaCore employs 210 people globally and manages 58 active enterprise contracts. Its product suite spans six platforms:

- **FlowCore** – Visual workflow automation builder
- **DataBridge** – Real-time data pipeline and integration platform
- **InsightIQ** – Business intelligence and analytics dashboard
- **ComplianceGuard** – Automated regulatory compliance monitoring
- **TalentFlow** – HR process automation and onboarding
- **NexaAssist** – AI-powered internal helpdesk and knowledge management

NexaCore serves clients across logistics, healthcare administration, financial services, and retail, ranging from regional operators to Fortune 500 enterprises. The company is headquartered in Austin, Texas, with offices in Chicago, Toronto, London, and Lisbon.
