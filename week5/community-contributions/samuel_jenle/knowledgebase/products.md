# NexaCore Products

NexaCore offers six enterprise SaaS platforms, each targeting a distinct operational pain point. All products are cloud-native, API-first, and integrate with the broader NexaCore ecosystem.

---

## FlowCore
**Category:** Workflow Automation  
**Launch Year:** 2018  
**Pricing:** $1,200–$8,000/month depending on seats and workflow volume

FlowCore is NexaCore's flagship product. It provides a no-code/low-code drag-and-drop interface for building, deploying, and monitoring business workflows. Key features include:
- 200+ pre-built connectors (Salesforce, SAP, NetSuite, Slack, Microsoft 365, etc.)
- Conditional branching, approval chains, and SLA tracking
- Real-time workflow monitoring and alerting
- Role-based access control and audit logging

**Target Sectors:** Logistics, Healthcare Administration, Financial Services  
**Active Contracts:** 22

---

## DataBridge
**Category:** Data Integration & Pipelines  
**Launch Year:** 2020  
**Pricing:** $2,000–$15,000/month based on data volume and connectors

DataBridge enables enterprises to build real-time and batch data pipelines across cloud, on-premise, and hybrid environments. Features include:
- Low-latency streaming via Kafka and Kinesis integrations
- Schema mapping, transformation, and data quality monitoring
- Support for 150+ data sources including databases, APIs, and file systems
- End-to-end data lineage tracking

**Target Sectors:** Retail, Financial Services, Logistics  
**Active Contracts:** 11

---

## InsightIQ
**Category:** Business Intelligence & Analytics  
**Launch Year:** 2021  
**Pricing:** $800–$5,000/month per workspace

InsightIQ is NexaCore's analytics layer, turning operational data into actionable dashboards and reports. Features include:
- Drag-and-drop dashboard builder with 50+ chart types
- AI-generated narrative summaries of KPI changes
- Scheduled reports and email delivery
- Native integration with FlowCore and DataBridge

**Target Sectors:** All sectors  
**Active Contracts:** 9

---

## ComplianceGuard
**Category:** Regulatory Compliance Automation  
**Launch Year:** 2022  
**Pricing:** $3,000–$20,000/month based on regulatory frameworks monitored

ComplianceGuard continuously monitors enterprise operations against regulatory requirements and flags violations before they become incidents. Features include:
- Pre-built rule sets for HIPAA, GDPR, SOX, and ISO 27001
- Automated evidence collection for audits
- Risk scoring and remediation playbooks
- Integration with HR, finance, and IT systems

**Target Sectors:** Healthcare, Financial Services  
**Active Contracts:** 7

---

## TalentFlow
**Category:** HR Process Automation  
**Launch Year:** 2023  
**Pricing:** $500–$3,500/month based on employee count

TalentFlow automates the full employee lifecycle from recruiting to offboarding. Features include:
- Automated onboarding checklists and IT provisioning triggers
- PTO and leave management workflows
- Performance review cycle automation
- Integration with Workday, BambooHR, and ADP

**Target Sectors:** All sectors  
**Active Contracts:** 5

---

## NexaAssist
**Category:** AI-Powered Internal Helpdesk  
**Launch Year:** 2024  
**Pricing:** $1,000–$6,000/month based on users and knowledge base size

NexaAssist is NexaCore's newest product, embedding a RAG-powered AI assistant into enterprise operations. Employees can ask questions and get instant answers grounded in company documentation, policies, and SOPs. Features include:
- Ingests PDFs, Word docs, Confluence pages, and Notion workspaces
- Cites sources in every response
- Escalation routing to human agents when confidence is low
- Usage analytics and unanswered question tracking

**Target Sectors:** All sectors  
**Active Contracts:** 4
