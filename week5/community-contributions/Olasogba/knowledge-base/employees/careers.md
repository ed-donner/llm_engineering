## Cadet Pilot Program

### Airpeace Wings Academy

**Program Overview:**
Transform your dream of becoming a pilot into reality with our comprehensive cadet program.

**Duration:** 18-24 months

**Training Locations:**
- Ground school: Lagos
- Flight training: Partner schools (South Africa, USA)

**Program Includes:**
- Private Pilot License (PPL)
- Commercial Pilot License (CPL)
- Instrument Rating (IR)
- Multi-Engine Rating (MER)
- Type rating on Airpeace aircraft

**Requirements:**
- Age: 18-28 years
- Bachelor's degree (any discipline)
- Class 1 Medical Certificate
- ICAO English Level 4 minimum
- Height: 160-195cm

**Cost:** Partially sponsored (bond agreement required)

**Career Path:** First Officer upon successful completion

## Application Process

### How to Apply

1. **Online Application**
   Visit: careers.flyairpeace.com
   - Create account
   - Complete profile
   - Upload documents
   - Submit application

2. **Required Documents**
   - Updated CV
   - Cover letter
   - Academic certificates
   - Professional certifications
   - Valid ID
   - Passport photograph

3. **Selection Process**
   - Application screening
   - Online assessment
   - Interview (virtual or in-person)
   - Reference checks
   - Medical examination
   - Offer and onboarding

### Application Tips
- Tailor CV to specific role
- Highlight relevant experience
- Demonstrate passion for aviation
- Research Airpeace before interview
