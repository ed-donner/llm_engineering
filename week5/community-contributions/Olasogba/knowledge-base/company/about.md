# About Airpeace Nigeria

## Company Overview

**Airpeace Limited** is Nigeria's largest indigenous airline, headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria. Founded in 2013 by Chief Allen Onyema, the airline has grown to become a major player in African aviation.

## Mission Statement

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable air transportation services across Nigeria, Africa, and beyond while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and operational excellence.

## Vision

To be Africa's leading airline, connecting the continent to the world through superior service, innovation, and commitment to excellence.

## Core Values

- **Safety First**: Uncompromising commitment to passenger and crew safety
- **Customer Focus**: Putting passengers at the heart of everything we do
- **Integrity**: Honest and transparent business practices
- **Innovation**: Embracing technology and modern solutions
- **Excellence**: Striving for the highest standards in all operations

## Company History

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2013 | Company founded by Chief Allen Onyema |
| 2014 | Commenced domestic operations with 2 aircraft |
| 2016 | Expanded fleet to 10 aircraft |
| 2018 | Launched regional West African routes |
| 2020 | Commenced international operations to Dubai |
| 2022 | Fleet expanded to 30+ aircraft |
| 2024 | Launched routes to London and Johannesburg |

Airpeace operates a modern mixed fleet including:
- Boeing 777-300ER (Long-haul international)
- Boeing 737-800 (Domestic and regional)
- Embraer E195-E2 (Short-haul routes)
