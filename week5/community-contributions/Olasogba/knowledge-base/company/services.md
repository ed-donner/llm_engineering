# Airpeace Services

## Passenger Services

### Cabin Classes

#### Business Class
- Premium leather seats with 42-inch pitch
- Priority check-in and boarding
- Complimentary lounge access
- Gourmet Nigerian and international cuisine
- Personal entertainment system
- Extra baggage allowance (40kg checked, 10kg cabin)

#### Economy Class
- Comfortable seats with 32-inch pitch
- Complimentary meals on flights over 2 hours
- In-flight entertainment on select aircraft
- Standard baggage allowance (23kg checked, 7kg cabin)

### Special Services

- **Wheelchair Assistance**: Available at all airports
- **Unaccompanied Minors**: Children ages 5-12 traveling alone
- **Pet Transport**: Approved carriers in cabin or cargo
- **Medical Equipment**: Oxygen concentrators and medical devices
- **Religious Meals**: Halal and kosher options available

## Cargo Services

### Air Cargo
- Express delivery across Nigeria
- Regional cargo to West Africa
- International cargo to Dubai, London, Johannesburg
- Perishables handling
- Dangerous goods (approved categories)

### Charter Services
- VIP charter flights
- Group charter for events
- Cargo charter services
- Medical evacuation flights
