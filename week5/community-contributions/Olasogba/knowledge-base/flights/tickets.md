# Airpeace Ticket Information

## E-Ticket Overview

All Airpeace tickets are issued electronically (e-tickets). No paper tickets are required.

## E-Ticket Components

### Ticket Number Format
**Structure:** 627-0123456789

| Component | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| 627 | Airpeace airline code |
| 10 digits | Unique ticket identifier |

### Booking Reference (PNR)
- 6 alphanumeric characters
- Example: ABC123
- Used for online check-in and manage booking

## Ticket Types

### Standard E-Ticket
- Issued for regular bookings
- Valid for named passenger only
- Includes all flight segments

### Open Ticket
- Flexible date travel
- Available on Flex and Business fares
- Valid for 12 months from issue

### Award Ticket (Miles)
- Redeemed using Airpeace Rewards miles
- Taxes and fees payable in cash
- Subject to award seat availability

### Corporate Ticket
- Issued via corporate portal
- May include corporate discount
- Billing to company account

## Ticket Validity

### Standard Validity
| Ticket Type | Validity Period |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Confirmed booking | Travel dates booked |
| Open ticket | 12 months from issue |
| Award ticket | 12 months from issue |

### Extension Policy
- Extensions may be granted for:
  - Medical emergencies (with documentation)
  - Airline schedule changes
  - Force majeure events
- Contact customer service for extension requests

## Infant and Child Tickets

### Infant (Under 2 years)
- 10% of adult fare
- No seat (sits on lap)
- No baggage allowance
- Birth certificate required

### Child (2-11 years)
- 75% of adult fare
- Own seat
- Full baggage allowance
- Birth certificate may be requested

### Unaccompanied Minor (5-12 years)
- Full adult fare + ₦15,000 service fee
- Mandatory supervision service
- Forms required at check-in

## Ticket Changes and Reissue

### Voluntary Changes

| Fare Type | Change Fee | Fare Difference |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Promo | Not permitted | - |
| Saver | ₦5,000 | Pay difference |
| Flex | Free | Pay difference |
| Business | Free | Pay difference |

### Involuntary Changes (Airline-initiated)
- No change fee
- Rebooking on next available
- Upgrade when available
- Refund option

### Reissue Process
1. Contact reservations or visit website
2. Provide PNR and passenger details
3. Select new flights
4. Pay applicable fees/difference
5. Receive updated e-ticket

## No-Show Policy

### Consequences of No-Show
- Ticket becomes void
- No refund (including Flex fares)
- Return segments cancelled
- Rebooking not guaranteed

### Avoiding No-Show
- Cancel online before departure
- Contact reservations if unable to travel
- Convert to travel credit if eligible

## E-Ticket Retrieval

### Lost Confirmation?
1. **Online:** Visit flyairpeace.com > Manage Booking
2. **Email:** Request from reservations@flyairpeace.com
3. **Phone:** Call +234 1 280 4501

### Required Information
- Passenger full name
- Travel dates
- Email or phone used for booking

## Ticket Verification

### How to Verify Your Ticket
1. Visit www.flyairpeace.com
2. Click "Manage Booking"
3. Enter PNR and passenger surname
4. View ticket details

### Fraud Prevention
- Book only through official channels
- Verify agent accreditation
- Check ticket number starts with 627
- Confirm via Airpeace website
