# KUCCPS: Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service

KUCCPS is the body that places KCSE candidates into degree, diploma, and certificate programmes in Kenyan universities and colleges. Placement is based on merit (cluster points), programme capacity, and choices submitted by the candidate.

## Cluster points formula

The weighted cluster points used for placement are computed as:

**C = √((r/R) × (t/T)) × 48**

- **r** = raw cluster score (sum of the best four cluster subject points, max 48)
- **R** = 48 (maximum raw cluster)
- **t** = aggregate (sum of best seven subject points, max 84)
- **T** = 84 (maximum aggregate)

So both your cluster-subject performance and your overall best-seven performance matter. A student with high aggregate and high cluster subjects gets a high weighted cluster (C).

## Clusters (subject groups)

- **Cluster 1** – Engineering & Physical Sciences: English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry (e.g. Engineering, Computer Science, Architecture).
- **Cluster 2** – Biological Sciences: English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry (e.g. Medicine, Pharmacy, Nursing, Agriculture).
- **Cluster 3** – Business & Economics: English, Mathematics, Kiswahili, Business Studies (e.g. Commerce, Economics, Actuarial Science).
- **Cluster 4A** – Education (Science): English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology (e.g. B.Ed Science, B.Ed Mathematics).
- **Cluster 4B** – Education (Arts): English, Mathematics, Kiswahili, Geography, History, CRE (e.g. B.Ed Arts, B.Ed Languages).
- **Cluster 5** – Arts & Social Sciences: English, Kiswahili, Geography, History, CRE (e.g. Law, Journalism, Social Work).

## Interpreting your score

- **Weighted cluster ≥ 40**: Very strong; you qualify for most competitive programmes in that cluster.
- **Weighted cluster 30–39**: Good; you qualify for many programmes.
- **Weighted cluster 20–29**: Moderate; you may qualify for some programmes; check cut-offs.
- **Weighted cluster &lt; 20**: Consider improving grades or exploring other clusters/courses.

Cut-off points vary by programme and institution. Always check the official KUCCPS portal for current cut-offs before making choices.
