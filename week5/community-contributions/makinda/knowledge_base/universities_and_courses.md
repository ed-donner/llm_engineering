# Kenyan universities and sample programmes by cluster

Below are sample universities and programmes that typically fall under each KUCCPS cluster. Cut-off points change every year; use these as guidance and confirm on the KUCCPS portal.

## Cluster 1 – Engineering & Physical Sciences

Programmes: B.Sc. Engineering (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, etc.), B.Arch, B.Sc. Computer Science, B.Sc. Actuarial Science (math-heavy), B.Sc. Physics, B.Sc. Chemistry.

**Sample universities (public):** University of Nairobi (UoN), Kenyatta University (KU), Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT), Moi University, Technical University of Kenya (TUK), Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT).

**Sample universities (private):** Strathmore University, United States International University – Africa (USIU-A), Kenyatta University (private programmes).

Typical guidance: Strong Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry (and English) improve your cluster points. High weighted cluster (e.g. 38+) often required for competitive engineering and computer science slots.

## Cluster 2 – Biological Sciences

Programmes: Medicine (MBChB), Pharmacy, Nursing, B.Sc. Agriculture, B.Sc. Veterinary Medicine, B.Sc. Food Science, B.Sc. Environmental Science.

**Sample universities (public):** University of Nairobi (UoN), Moi University, Kenyatta University (KU), JKUAT, Egerton University, Maseno University.

**Sample universities (private):** Mount Kenya University (MKU), Kenyatta University (private programmes).

Typical guidance: Biology and Chemistry are critical. Medicine and Pharmacy have very high cut-offs; Nursing and Agriculture have a wider range. Consider related programmes (e.g. Public Health, Biomedical Sciences) if cut-offs are not met.

## Cluster 3 – Business & Economics

Programmes: B.Com, B.Sc. Economics, B.Sc. Actuarial Science, B.Sc. Finance, Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting, Finance, Marketing).

**Sample universities (public):** University of Nairobi (UoN), Kenyatta University (KU), Moi University, Strathmore University (private), USIU-A, JKUAT.

**Sample universities (private):** Strathmore University, USIU-A, MKU, KCA University.

Typical guidance: English and Mathematics are weighted heavily. Good cluster points (e.g. 32+) open many business and economics programmes across public and private universities.

## Cluster 4A & 4B – Education (Science / Arts)

Programmes: B.Ed (Science), B.Ed (Mathematics), B.Ed (Arts), B.Ed (Languages), Diploma in Education.

**Sample universities (public):** Kenyatta University (KU), Moi University, Maseno University, Egerton University, University of Nairobi (UoN).

**Sample universities (private):** MKU, Catholic University of Eastern Africa (CUEA).

Typical guidance: Education programmes train teachers; cut-offs are often more accessible than pure science or engineering. Choose Science vs Arts cluster based on the subjects you want to teach.

## Cluster 5 – Arts & Social Sciences

Programmes: LL.B (Law), Journalism, Social Work, Public Administration, Sociology, Political Science, International Relations, Communication.

**Sample universities (public):** University of Nairobi (UoN), Moi University, Kenyatta University (KU), Maseno University.

**Sample universities (private):** Strathmore University, USIU-A, MKU, KCA University.

Typical guidance: Law (LL.B) usually has high cut-offs. Journalism, Social Work, and Public Administration are offered at many institutions with varying cut-offs. Strong English and Kiswahili help your cluster points.

## Important note

Always verify current cut-off points and programme codes on the official KUCCPS website before submitting your choices. This document is for guidance only; at most four universities are typically suggested per student based on cluster and course of interest.
