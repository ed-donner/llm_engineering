# About Marriage Atony LLM

Marriage Atony LLM is an AI-powered legal assistant specializing in marriage annulment, divorce, and reconciliation cases. The platform provides legal research, case analysis, and outcome prediction to support attorneys and clients in navigating complex marital legal matters.

## Mission
To empower legal professionals and individuals with advanced AI tools for fair, efficient, and informed resolution of marriage-related cases.

## Core Values
- Justice
- Empathy
- Confidentiality
- Innovation
