# Ashir General Hospital — Overview

## At a Glance
- **Founded:** 2004
- **Founder & Chair of the Board:** Dr. Imran Ashir, MD, FACS
- **Chief Executive Officer:** Farah Qureshi
- **Chief Medical Officer:** Dr. Samuel Okafor, MD
- **Licensed Beds:** 520
- **Annual Outpatient Visits:** ~180,000
- **Annual Inpatient Admissions:** ~32,000
- **Annual ER Visits:** ~95,000
- **Annual Surgical Procedures:** ~14,500
- **Active Medical Staff:** 62 attending physicians, 48 residents, 210 nurses

## Campuses
1. **Main Campus** — 360 beds, ICUs, operating theaters, cath lab, oncology infusion center.
2. **Women & Children's Wing** — 120 beds, Labor & Delivery, NICU Level III, Pediatric ICU.
3. **Ashir Community Clinic** — Outpatient primary care, preventive medicine, vaccination, diabetes education.

## Clinical Departments
Cardiology, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Oncology, Hematology, Neurology, Neurosurgery,
Endocrinology, Pulmonology, Gastroenterology, Nephrology, Orthopedics, General Surgery,
Pediatrics, Neonatology, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Psychiatry, Dermatology, Radiology,
Pathology, Anesthesiology, Emergency Medicine, Infectious Disease, Family Medicine,
Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation.

## Quality & Accreditation
- Joint Commission International (JCI) accredited since 2014.
- Recognized "Center of Excellence" for Diabetes Care (2021) and Cardiac Care (2023).
- Patient satisfaction score: 92% (2025).
- 30-day hospital readmission rate: 8.4% (below national average of 15%).

## Teaching & Research
Ashir runs residency programs in Internal Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, OB-GYN, Emergency Medicine, Psychiatry, and Radiology. The hospital's research arm, the Ashir Institute for Clinical Research, publishes 40+ peer-reviewed papers annually and currently participates in 18 active clinical trials.
