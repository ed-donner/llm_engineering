# Departments at Ashir General Hospital

Ashir operates 24 clinical departments organized into five care lines. Each department is led by a department chair who reports to the Chief Medical Officer.

## 1. Cardiovascular Services
- **Cardiology** — Chair: Dr. Rehan Malik. 12 cardiologists, 3 electrophysiologists.
- **Cardiothoracic Surgery** — Chair: Dr. Ana Duarte. Performs CABG, valve replacement, and TAVR.
- **Cardiac Cath Lab** — 2 suites, ~1,200 procedures/year.

## 2. Oncology & Hematology
- **Medical Oncology** — Chair: Dr. Priya Nair. Breast, lung, colorectal, and hematologic cancers.
- **Radiation Oncology** — 2 linear accelerators, IMRT and SBRT capability.
- **Infusion Center** — 22 chairs, ~9,000 infusion visits/year.

## 3. Women & Children
- **Obstetrics & Gynecology** — Chair: Dr. Lina Haddad. 4,200+ deliveries/year.
- **Neonatology (NICU Level III)** — 28 bassinets.
- **Pediatrics** — 30-bed general ward plus an 8-bed PICU.

## 4. Medical & Surgical Specialties
- **Endocrinology** — Diabetes Center of Excellence, led by Dr. Kenji Tanaka.
- **Neurology & Stroke Program** — 24/7 stroke team, tPA door-to-needle <45 min.
- **Neurosurgery** — Chair: Dr. Arjun Bose.
- **Gastroenterology** — Endoscopy suite with 4 rooms.
- **Nephrology** — 18-station outpatient dialysis unit.
- **Pulmonology** — Sleep lab and interventional bronchoscopy.
- **Orthopedics** — Joint replacement, sports medicine, trauma.
- **General Surgery** — Including minimally invasive and bariatric surgery.
- **Urology, Dermatology, ENT, Ophthalmology** — Outpatient-focused clinics.

## 5. Support & Diagnostic Services
- **Emergency Medicine** — Level II Trauma Center.
- **Radiology** — MRI (3T and 1.5T), CT, PET-CT, ultrasound, interventional radiology.
- **Pathology & Laboratory Medicine** — 2.2M tests/year.
- **Pharmacy** — 24/7 inpatient pharmacy with clinical pharmacists on all care teams.
- **Anesthesiology & Perioperative Services**
- **Psychiatry & Behavioral Health** — 20-bed inpatient unit and outpatient clinic.
- **Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation** — 25-bed inpatient rehab unit.
- **Infectious Disease** — Runs the hospital's antimicrobial stewardship program.
