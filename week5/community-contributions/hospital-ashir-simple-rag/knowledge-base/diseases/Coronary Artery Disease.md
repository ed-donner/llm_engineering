# Condition Summary

# Coronary Artery Disease (CAD)

## Overview

Coronary Artery Disease is the single highest-volume program at Ashir General Hospital. The Cardiology department, chaired by Dr. Rehan Malik, treats CAD across the full severity spectrum — from outpatient primary and secondary prevention to acute myocardial infarction in the Emergency Department and complex percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI) in the Cardiac Cath Lab.

Ashir's Cardiac Cath Lab performs over **1,200 procedures per year** and is a regional referral center for STEMI and NSTEMI patients.

## Services Offered

### 1. Non-invasive Cardiac Testing
Stress ECG, exercise and pharmacologic stress echo, nuclear myocardial perfusion imaging, and cardiac CT angiography.

### 2. 24/7 STEMI Program
Dedicated on-call interventional team; door-to-balloon time consistently averages **52 minutes**, well below the 90-minute national benchmark.

### 3. Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI)
Full range including complex PCI (CTO, bifurcation, left main), using drug-eluting stents, IVUS, and FFR guidance.

### 4. Cardiothoracic Surgery
When surgical revascularization is indicated, Dr. Ana Duarte's CT surgery team performs CABG (including multivessel and off-pump procedures) in collaboration with Cardiology.

### 5. Cardiac Rehabilitation
12-week outpatient cardiac rehab program with supervised exercise, nutrition counseling, and behavior change support. Enrollment rates after MI at Ashir are 74%, far above the national average of 30%.

### 6. Preventive Cardiology Clinic
Dedicated clinic for lipid disorders, advanced lipid testing, PCSK9 inhibitors, and cardiovascular risk stratification.

### 7. Structural Heart Program
TAVR, MitraClip, and left atrial appendage closure for eligible patients.

### 8. Heart Failure & CAD Co-management
Tight integration with the heart failure clinic for patients with ischemic cardiomyopathy.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **30-day mortality after STEMI PCI:** 2.1% (national avg ~5%)
- **Same-day discharge after elective PCI:** 48% of cases
- **Cardiac rehab enrollment after MI:** 74%
- **PCI-related complications:** <1% major adverse events

## Service Tiers

- **Acute Care:** All emergency and STEMI services are covered under standard admission protocols.
- **Ashir Cardiac Wellness Program:** $185/month — quarterly preventive cardiology visits, lipid monitoring, stress test as needed.
- **Comprehensive Heart Health Package:** $1,400/year — includes cardiac CT calcium score, full preventive cardiology consultation, nutrition consult, and cardiac rehab access.

Ashir's CAD program brings together interventional, surgical, and preventive cardiology under one roof, providing seamless, evidence-based care at every stage of the disease.
