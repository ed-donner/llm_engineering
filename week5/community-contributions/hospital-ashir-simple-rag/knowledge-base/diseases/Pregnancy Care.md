# Pregnancy Care & High-Risk Obstetrics

## Overview

Ashir's Women & Children's Wing is home to a full-service Obstetrics program, from low-risk pregnancy care to advanced Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM). The program is chaired by Dr. Lina Haddad and delivers **~4,200 babies per year**, supported by a Level III NICU and dedicated obstetric anesthesia coverage.

Although pregnancy is not a "disease," it is one of the highest-volume clinical pathways at Ashir and a core part of our knowledge base, covering routine prenatal care, gestational diabetes, hypertensive disorders of pregnancy, and preterm labor.

## Services Offered

### 1. Prenatal Care
Structured 10-12 visit schedule including first-trimester dating, cell-free fetal DNA (NIPT), anatomy ultrasound at 20 weeks, and third-trimester growth assessment.

### 2. Maternal-Fetal Medicine Clinic
Specialized care for high-risk pregnancies: advanced maternal age, multifetal pregnancies, preeclampsia, chronic hypertension, pregestational diabetes, and fetal anomalies.

### 3. Gestational Diabetes Program
Co-managed by OB/GYN and Endocrinology (Dr. Kenji Tanaka). Includes diabetes education, home glucose monitoring, nutrition counseling, and insulin initiation when needed.

### 4. Labor & Delivery
6 L&D rooms and 2 dedicated obstetric operating rooms. 24/7 obstetric anesthesia and neonatology presence for high-risk deliveries.

### 5. Cesarean Section Program
Evidence-based enhanced recovery after cesarean (ERAC) pathway — reducing opioid use by 60% and shortening length of stay to a median of 2.1 days.

### 6. Level III Neonatal Intensive Care Unit
28-bassinet NICU capable of caring for infants born as early as 24 weeks' gestation.

### 7. Safe Motherhood Initiative
Free prenatal care program for uninsured expectant mothers, founded by Dr. Haddad. Covers all prenatal visits, ultrasounds, and delivery.

### 8. Postpartum & Lactation Support
6-week postpartum visit, postpartum depression screening (integrated with Psychiatry), and certified lactation consultants on staff.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **Preterm birth rate:** 8.1% (national average ~10%)
- **NICU admission rate (overall):** 9.2%
- **Maternal mortality in program:** 0 cases in the last 4 years
- **Cesarean rate (primary, low-risk):** 18% (goal <23%)
- **Postpartum depression screening rate:** 98%

## Service Tiers

- **Standard Prenatal Package:** Covered by most insurers — all routine prenatal visits, ultrasounds, and delivery.
- **Premium MFM Package:** $4,500 out-of-pocket supplement — additional MFM visits, extended ultrasound imaging, and fetal echocardiography.
- **Safe Motherhood Initiative:** Free for qualifying uninsured patients.
- **Ashir Postpartum Recovery Program:** $280/month — home nurse visits, lactation support, postpartum mental health check-ins for 12 weeks after delivery.
