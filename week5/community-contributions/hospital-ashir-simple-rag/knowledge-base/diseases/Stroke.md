# Condition Summary

# Stroke (Cerebrovascular Accident)

## Overview

Ashir General Hospital is a certified Primary Stroke Center and operates a 24/7 stroke response team co-led by Neurology (Dr. Anjali Sen) and Neurosurgery (Dr. Arjun Bose). The program covers ischemic stroke, hemorrhagic stroke, and transient ischemic attacks (TIA), with tight integration across the ED, radiology, neurology, neurosurgery, ICU, and rehabilitation services.

Ashir treats approximately **600 acute stroke patients per year**, with an additional 300 TIA evaluations.

## Services Offered

### 1. 24/7 Stroke Alert System
On arrival to the ED, a "Code Stroke" activates the neurology team, CT scanner, and pharmacy simultaneously. Average door-to-needle time: **42 minutes** (national target: <60 minutes).

### 2. IV Thrombolysis (tPA / Tenecteplase)
Administered in eligible patients presenting within 4.5 hours of symptom onset.

### 3. Mechanical Thrombectomy
Interventional radiology and Dr. Bose's neurosurgery team provide endovascular clot retrieval for large-vessel occlusions, with on-call coverage 24/7.

### 4. Hemorrhagic Stroke Care
Neurosurgical management of intracerebral hemorrhage, subarachnoid hemorrhage (aneurysm coiling or clipping), and decompressive craniectomy when indicated.

### 5. Stroke Unit
8-bed dedicated stroke unit with 1:3 nursing ratio, continuous telemetry, and early mobilization protocols.

### 6. TIA Clinic
Rapid-access TIA clinic — patients seen within 24 hours of referral for vascular imaging and risk-factor optimization.

### 7. Inpatient & Outpatient Rehabilitation
25-bed rehab unit with physical, occupational, and speech therapy. Outpatient rehab continues post-discharge for up to 12 weeks.

### 8. Secondary Prevention Clinic
Coordinated anticoagulation management (especially for AFib-related stroke), statin optimization, blood pressure control, and lifestyle counseling.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **Door-to-needle time (tPA):** Median 42 min
- **Door-to-groin puncture (thrombectomy):** Median 68 min
- **90-day modified Rankin Scale 0-2 after ischemic stroke:** 62%
- **30-day stroke readmission rate:** 6.1%

## Service Tiers

- **Emergency Care:** All acute stroke care is fully activated on presentation regardless of insurance.
- **Ashir Stroke Prevention Program:** $210/month — for patients at elevated risk (prior TIA, AFib, carotid disease); includes quarterly neurology visits, annual vascular imaging, and medication management.
- **Post-Stroke Recovery Package:** $3,500 — 12-week bundled outpatient rehab + neurology follow-up + secondary prevention clinic.
