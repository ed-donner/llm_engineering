# Condition Summary

# Asthma (Pediatric & Adult)

## Overview

Asthma is the most common chronic disease seen in Ashir's Pediatric and Pulmonology clinics. The pediatric program is led by Dr. Elena Rossi at the Women & Children's Wing, while the adult program is managed by the Pulmonology division. Together, the two clinics follow more than **2,400 active asthma patients** across the region.

## Services Offered

### 1. Diagnosis & Classification
Spirometry with pre- and post-bronchodilator testing, exhaled nitric oxide (FeNO), methacholine challenge testing, and allergy testing through our in-house allergist.

### 2. Written Asthma Action Plans
Every patient (pediatric and adult) leaves with a personalized, color-coded action plan — green / yellow / red zones with exact medication instructions.

### 3. Controller & Rescue Therapy
Full access to ICS, ICS/LABA combinations, LAMAs, leukotriene receptor antagonists, and short-acting rescue inhalers.

### 4. Severe Asthma Biologics Clinic
For patients with uncontrolled severe asthma despite step-up therapy. Ashir offers omalizumab, mepolizumab, benralizumab, dupilumab, and tezepelumab, with dedicated nursing support for injection clinics.

### 5. Allergen Immunotherapy
Subcutaneous and sublingual immunotherapy for dust mite, pollen, and pet dander allergies (in partnership with Allergy).

### 6. Asthma Education Program
Group classes for families and patients covering inhaler technique, trigger avoidance, and exacerbation management. Led by certified asthma educators.

### 7. School & Sports Integration
School nurse liaison program that delivers up-to-date asthma action plans and spare rescue inhalers to schools across the district.

### 8. ER Exacerbation Pathway
Dedicated pediatric ER asthma pathway including early systemic steroids, structured bronchodilator therapy, and warm hand-off to outpatient clinic within 3 days of discharge.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **Pediatric asthma ER revisit rate (30 days):** 6% (down from 17% in 2022)
- **Pediatric asthma admission rate:** Declined 28% between 2022 and 2025
- **Adult severe asthma biologics — proportion with 50%+ reduction in exacerbations:** 81%
- **Patient/family satisfaction:** 4.9 / 5 (pediatric clinic)

## Service Tiers

- **Standard Asthma Care:** Covered under routine visits; includes spirometry, education, and all first-line and step-up therapy.
- **Severe Asthma Membership:** $220/month — includes monthly visits, biologic administration, and care coordination for patients on biologic therapy.
- **School Asthma Program:** Free community service — Ashir supplies action plans, spare inhalers, and nurse training for partnering schools.
