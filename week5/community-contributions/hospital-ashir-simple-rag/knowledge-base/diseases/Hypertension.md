# Condition Summary

# Hypertension

## Overview

Hypertension is the most prevalent chronic disease at Ashir General Hospital. More than **9,500 patients** are actively managed across our Internal Medicine, Cardiology, and Community Clinic teams. The Ashir Hypertension Program combines evidence-based medication management with intensive lifestyle coaching, 24-hour ambulatory monitoring, and close coordination with nephrology and cardiology for complex cases.

## Services Offered

### 1. Diagnosis & Staging
In-clinic automated BP measurement, 24-hour ambulatory blood pressure monitoring (ABPM), and home BP diary review. Secondary causes of hypertension are screened where clinically appropriate.

### 2. Medication Management
Full access to first-line (ACEi/ARBs, thiazide diuretics, calcium channel blockers), second-line, and fourth-line agents (spironolactone, centrally-acting agents). Clinical pharmacists review complex regimens.

### 3. Resistant Hypertension Clinic
Dedicated quarterly clinic in Cardiology for patients on 3+ agents at maximum doses. Work-up includes renal artery imaging, plasma aldosterone/renin ratio, and sleep apnea screening.

### 4. Lifestyle Coaching
Nutrition counseling (DASH diet), structured exercise prescription, weight-loss support, and sodium-reduction coaching.

### 5. Home BP Monitoring Program
Patients are enrolled in a remote monitoring program with a validated BP cuff, uploading readings via the Ashir patient portal. Alerts are reviewed by clinical pharmacists, who can adjust therapy under a collaborative practice agreement.

### 6. Integrated Cardiovascular Risk Assessment
10-year ASCVD risk calculation at every annual visit, with lipid and diabetes screening.

### 7. Pregnancy Hypertension Clinic
Co-run with Maternal-Fetal Medicine (Dr. Lina Haddad) for chronic hypertension in pregnancy, gestational hypertension, and preeclampsia.

### 8. Complication Screening
ECG, echocardiogram as indicated, microalbumin, fundoscopy, and eGFR monitoring at regular intervals.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **Patients achieving BP < 140/90 mmHg:** 82%
- **Patients achieving BP < 130/80 mmHg (high-risk target):** 61%
- **Hospital admissions for hypertensive urgency/emergency:** Reduced 29% since 2022
- **Home BP program enrollment:** 1,400+ patients

## Service Tiers

- **Standard Outpatient Care:** Covered under primary care visits. Includes medication titration and annual screening.
- **Ashir BP Home Monitoring Program:** $45/month — includes loaner BP cuff, portal integration, and pharmacist reviews.
- **Resistant Hypertension Package:** $1,100 — full secondary-cause work-up (ABPM, aldosterone/renin ratio, renal imaging, sleep study referral).

## Research & Roadmap

- **Q1 2027:** Launch of renal denervation service for resistant hypertension.
- **Q3 2026:** AI-powered BP trend analysis added to the Ashir patient portal.
- **2027:** Participation in trials evaluating zilebesiran (siRNA therapy for hypertension).

Ashir's hypertension program aims to reduce long-term cardiovascular and renal complications through aggressive, evidence-based, patient-centered management.
