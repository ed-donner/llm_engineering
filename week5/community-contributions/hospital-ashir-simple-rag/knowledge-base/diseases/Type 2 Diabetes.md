# Condition Summary

# Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus

## Overview

Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (T2DM) is one of the most common chronic diseases managed at Ashir General Hospital. Ashir's Diabetes Center of Excellence, led by Dr. Kenji Tanaka, provides end-to-end care for T2DM patients — from initial diagnosis and lifestyle counseling to insulin management, complication screening, and advanced therapies such as GLP-1 agonists and continuous glucose monitoring.

Approximately **4,200 patients** with T2DM are actively managed across Ashir's Main Campus and Community Clinic, making it one of our highest-volume chronic-disease programs.

## Services Offered

### 1. Diagnosis & Initial Evaluation
Point-of-care HbA1c testing, diabetes type confirmation (including autoantibody testing when needed), and a structured intake consultation with the endocrinology team.

### 2. Medication Management
Full access to modern antidiabetic therapies including metformin, SGLT2 inhibitors, DPP-4 inhibitors, GLP-1 receptor agonists, and all forms of insulin. Complex regimens are co-managed by endocrinology and clinical pharmacy.

### 3. Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM)
Ashir is a certified training site for Dexcom and Freestyle Libre CGM devices. Patients receive hands-on training and remote data-sharing setup.

### 4. Diabetes Education Program
Group classes and 1:1 sessions taught by certified diabetes educators (CDEs). Curriculum includes nutrition, exercise, self-monitoring, hypoglycemia recognition, and sick-day rules.

### 5. Complication Screening
Annual retinal photography with Ophthalmology, urine microalbumin, ECG, monofilament foot exam, and lipid panels — all tracked in the patient's diabetes dashboard.

### 6. Cardiology Co-management
Every diabetic patient with ASCVD risk is offered a cardiology consultation with Dr. Rehan Malik's team to optimize statin and blood pressure therapy.

### 7. Behavioral Health Integration
Depression and diabetes distress screening at every endocrinology visit. Integrated referral to Dr. Noor Al-Sayed's team when indicated.

### 8. Insulin Pump & Automated Insulin Delivery (AID)
Ashir supports MiniMed 780G, Tandem t:slim X2, and Omnipod 5 systems, with dedicated pump-training nurses.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **Patients at target HbA1c (<7.0%):** 78% (vs. national avg of 52%)
- **Severe hypoglycemia admissions:** Reduced by 42% since 2022
- **Diabetic ketoacidosis readmissions:** Below 4%
- **Patient satisfaction:** 4.8 / 5

## Service Tiers

Ashir offers tiered diabetes care for different patient populations:

- **Standard Care:** Routine outpatient endocrinology, included in primary admission or insurance plan.
- **Intensive Management Program:** For complex cases — weekly touchpoints with the care team, CGM loaner, and nutritionist, billed per encounter or bundled at $1,800/quarter.
- **Ashir DiabetesPlus Membership:** $250/month out-of-pocket program for patients without insurance — includes unlimited visits, CGM sensors, and education materials.
