# Condition Summary

# Breast Cancer

## Overview

Breast cancer care at Ashir is delivered by the Ashir Cancer Center — a multidisciplinary team led by Dr. Priya Nair (Medical Oncology), with close partnership between surgical oncology, radiation oncology, pathology, and genetics. Ashir received Commission on Cancer accreditation in 2023 and serves as the regional referral center for early-stage through stage III breast cancer.

The Breast Cancer Program currently follows **850+ active patients** and performs around **260 new breast cancer diagnoses per year**.

## Services Offered

### 1. Screening & Early Detection
3D digital mammography at both the Main Campus and Community Clinic. Risk-based screening pathways including MRI for high-risk patients (BRCA carriers, >20% lifetime risk).

### 2. Diagnostic Workup
Same-week biopsy scheduling, immunohistochemistry (ER/PR/HER2), Ki-67, and access to genomic assays (Oncotype DX, MammaPrint).

### 3. Surgical Oncology
Breast-conserving surgery, mastectomy, sentinel lymph node biopsy, and oncoplastic reconstruction. Average time from diagnosis to surgery: 18 days.

### 4. Systemic Therapy
Adjuvant and neoadjuvant chemotherapy, HER2-directed therapy (trastuzumab, pertuzumab, T-DXd), endocrine therapy, and CDK4/6 inhibitors for metastatic disease.

### 5. Radiation Oncology
Two linear accelerators supporting IMRT, hypofractionated whole-breast radiotherapy, and partial breast irradiation when indicated.

### 6. Genetic Counseling
Certified genetic counselors on-site. BRCA1/2, PALB2, and multi-gene panel testing offered.

### 7. Survivorship Program
Co-founded by Dr. Nair — structured follow-up, long-term toxicity monitoring, lifestyle and fertility counseling, and peer support groups.

### 8. Clinical Trials
Active enrollment in Phase II / III trials including adjuvant immunotherapy for triple-negative disease and novel antibody-drug conjugates.

## Outcomes (2025)

- **Time from abnormal mammogram to biopsy:** Median 6 days
- **Time from diagnosis to first treatment:** Median 22 days
- **5-year survival — Stage I:** 97%
- **5-year survival — Stage II:** 88%
- **Patient-reported quality-of-life score:** 4.6 / 5

## Service Tiers

- **Standard Cancer Care:** Fully covered through admission and insurance plans — includes all diagnostic and treatment services above.
- **Concierge Oncology Navigation:** $950/month — dedicated nurse navigator, expedited scheduling, and direct-line physician access.
- **Survivorship Membership:** $120/month — includes quarterly visits, lab monitoring, wellness classes, and access to the peer-support program.
